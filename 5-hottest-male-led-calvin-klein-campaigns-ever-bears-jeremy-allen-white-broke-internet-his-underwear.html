White’s toned muscles and abs against the backdrop of the iconic cityscape broke the internet, with fans (female ones especially) pouring in to leave their thirsty comments. “Want him in a way that is concerning to feminism,” one user wrote, while another fan simply said, “Yes chef!” referencing the actor’s role as the award-winning chef Carmy in the Hulu comedy-drama The Bear, for which he just won the Golden Globe for best actor – television series.

Calvin Klein underwear campaigns tend to have this knee-quivering, heart-racing effect on their audience – after all, this isn’t the brand’s first rodeo.

Here are five more of Calvin Klein’s hottest and most iconic underwear shoots from over the years.

1. Aaron Taylor-Johnson

The Bullet Train actor stripped down to his Calvin Klein underwear for the brand’s campaign in 2023, showing off his shredded body.

2. Mark Wahlberg

Wahlberg was only 21 and still went by his rapper name Marky Mark when he starred in a 1992 Calvin Klein campaign alongside a teenage Kate Moss. Wahlberg’s shoot is considered one of the most iconic adverts to date and its insane popularity is what led to the launch of his acting career.

3. Justin Bieber

Bieber has appeared in a number of campaigns for Calvin Klein, but the most memorable has to be his first shoot alongside supermodel Lara Stone in 2015.

4. Jamie Dornan

In 2009, before he starred in 50 Shades of Grey, Dornan appeared in a steamy campaign for Calvin Klein too.
