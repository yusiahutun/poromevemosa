Kelly Clarkson Is Married -- See Her Wedding Dress!

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Kelly Clarkson is no longer "Miss Independent" ... she's Mrs. Blackstock!

The original "Amerincan Idol" winner married talent manager Brandon Blackstock on Sunday at Blackberry Farms in Tennessee -- what Kelly calls "the most beautiful place ever."

Clarkson, 31, wore a gorgeous Temperly London wedding gown for her big day -- and she already shared some photos of her in her stunning lace dress.

With her hair done up and holding a bouquet of sunflowers, Kelly has a big smile on her face in the pic, as her new husband kisses her forehead.

This is the first marriage for Kelly. Brandon -- who is Reba McEntire's stepson -- has two kids (Savannah, 11, and Seth, 7) from his first marriage.

Congrats to the happy couple!
