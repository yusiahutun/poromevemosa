Jamie Chung Wedding Dress - Jamie Chung Marries Brian Greenburg

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 20, 2024

Jamie Chung tied the knot with Brian Greenberg on October 31 in Santa Barbara, sporting two gorgeous dresses during the ceremony, US Weekly reports. The first was reserved for the ceremony: a lace-sleeved Monique Lhuillier gown with a plunging v-neck. She wore her hair half up and completed the look with a cathedral-length veil and peep-toe pumps.

Media Platforms Design Team

But before the couple officially said "I do," Chung and Greenberg posed for photos, where she swapped her gown for a short lace dress by Jonathan Simkhai. She pulled her hair into a slick low pony and added a pair of white strappy sandals to finish the look. Contrary to his bride's switch-up, Greenberg stuck with one ensemble the entire ceremony: a navy suit, white button-down and black bow tie.

Congratulations to the happy couple!
