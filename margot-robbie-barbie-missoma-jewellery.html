Margot Robbie's favourite jewellery brand

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 1, 2024

If you've been keeping up with Barbie fever – and, let's face it, who hasn't? – you'll know that Margot Robbie has been sporting some incredible looks in tribute to the doll, from her outfits to her manicure. But now, it's her jewellery we're actually investing in: specifically, the affordable brand Robbie has been wearing both on and off screen.

In character as Barbie, Robbie sports the 'Ridged Heart Charm' necklace from Missoma, which features a chunky, heart-shaped pendant (handcrafted from brass with 18ct recycled gold plating) on a bobble chain, which she pairs with a lemon-yellow dress. The necklace is out of stock right now, but you can register to be notified when it becomes available – alternatively, you can shop similar styles from the brand, including this moonstone-set locket and this aqua enamel and gold heart-shaped pendant.

Courtesy of Missoma

Margot Robbie wearing Missoma in the new Barbie film

It's not the only Missoma piece in the film, either: worn by 'Celebrated Author Barbie', played by Alexandra Shipp, the 'Jelly Heart Gemstone Charm' necklace is a wonderfully kitsch piece to add to your jewellery box. With its sprinkling of pink, blue and yellow hearts (made from dyed mango and aqua chalcedony, blue, purple and pink quartz) set on an 18ct recycled gold-plated brass chain, it's pure Barbie cuteness.

It's no wonder Missoma makes a number of on-screen appearances in Barbie, given that it's been one of Robbie's best-loved brands for years. The actress wore the label's 'Medium Engravable Roman Arc Coin' necklace, part of a range made in collaboration with Lucy Williams, to a 'Women in Film' pre-Oscars party, and even sported the 'Large Horn' necklace while surfing in Malibu.

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Margot Robbie wearing Missoma at a ’Women in Film’ pre-Oscars event

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

And she's not the only famous tastemaker to favour the brand – the Princess of Wales has been spotted in several Missoma styles in the past, as have other A-listers including Hailey Bieber, Gigi Hadid and Emily Ratajkowski. So if you're looking to pick up a piece of Barbie memorabilia that will stand the test of time, consider this mid-priced label your go-to.
