What to write in a christening card: Best messages and wishes

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, February 26, 2024

Christening day is undeniably a precious occasion for the person being baptized as well as their family. Welcoming the individual to the Christian world may appear easy, but when the time comes, we may get at a loss for words and wonder what to write in a christening card or how to make them feel loved.

Watching your loved one get baptized and begin their journey through faith is an emotional and beautiful experience. At such moments it might be hard to know what to write in their baptism card. Some of the key features you ought to include are a personalized wish, a piece of future advice, or a verse from the scriptures for that occasion. Here are some of the best messages and wishes to help you express how you feel about the momentous event in the life of your loved one.
