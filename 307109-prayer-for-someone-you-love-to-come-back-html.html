Praying for someone you want to be with is not always easy, but you can do it and have faith and hope to be with them soon. You should pray for the Lord to be with them and to watch over them wherever they are. It is also important to request God to manifest and work on their lives. How do you pray for someone special? When praying, you must believe that God is already working on that person and is guiding them to a brighter life with you. Finally, give God the praise and the glory for the victory over the person's life as if it has already happened.
