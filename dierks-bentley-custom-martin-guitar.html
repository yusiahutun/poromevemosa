Dierks Bentley to Release Custom-Designed Guitar

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Music isn't the only thing Dierks Bentley is creating these days. The '5-1-5-0' singer is set to release a special Dierks Bentley Custom Signature Edition guitar, which will be made available to the public later this month.

“It’s kind of based on the song ‘Home,’ so there’s the herringbone right on the outside that’s red, white and blue," Bentley says of the guitar's design (quote via Country Music News Blog). "But it’s a little of Buck Owens' influence there as well, because I’m a huge Buck fan. There’s a phoenix on there that says where I’m from and just the specs on the guitar, are kinda custom to how I like them.”

Aside from the custom design, each guitar will also feature Bentley's autograph. The guitars will be manufactured by Martin Guitars,which has been producing top-quality music instruments since 1833.

Fans hoping to experience Bentley's skilled playing can catch the singer on the road this summer as he promotes his newest album, 'Home,' which features the No. 1 title track, and '5-1-5-0,' Bentley's current Top 5 single. Click here for tour dates and ticket information.
