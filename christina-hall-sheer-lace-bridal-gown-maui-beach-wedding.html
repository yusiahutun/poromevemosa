Christina Hall is a beachy goddess in sheer lace bridal gown for Maui wedding

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

The Flip or Flop star got married for the third time in 2022

    Christina Hall married her third husband Josh Hall in secret following a one-year relationship in early 2022.

    However, it wasn't until September 2022 that the couple released photos of their magical oceanside ceremony in Maui. The 38-year-old former Flip or Flop star, who fronted the show alongside her ex-husband Tarek El Moussa, was pictured hugging her realtor husband, 41, with the setting sun basking the happy newlyweds in a golden glow. 

    Christina looked radiant in a figure-hugging wedding dress in a champagne color with a sheer lace overlay, a structured bodice and a mermaid skirt. She wore her hair in loose beachy waves with the front section twisted into a braid, while her soft pastel pink bouquet rested on the rocks beside her feet. 

    Meanwhile, Josh looked dapper in a cream suit and a white shirt with a floral garland around his neck.

    "Shared vows in front of family and our close friends. Everything in life has led me to where I am right now, which is exactly where I want to be. My dream man on the dreamiest island. Maui holds a special place in my heart. What an amazing night filled with love," Christine captioned the photo.

    Her fans commented: "Beautiful! Congratulations!" and: "Most beautiful wedding picture ever!" Another added: "A beautiful moment and a beautiful couple."

    Fans may have spotted that the wedding dress Christina wore for her big day with her ex-husband Ant Anstead had a similar mermaid silhouette. She wore a lace gown with long sheer sleeves for a low-key ceremony in their backyard in Newport Beach, California.

    The Christina on the Coast star was previously married to Tarek from 2009 to 2018, during which time she welcomed children Taylor and Brayden, before tying the knot with Ant in 2018 and welcoming son Hudson.

    Her two sons accompanied her down the aisle for her wedding with Josh, and they looked cute in matching white shirts and cream pants with braces.

    Christina recently said she and Josh have no plans to expand their family, but praised his role as a stepfather to her kids. "We're done here. I can't even imagine that," she said on the subject of having another child. "I think my head would fall off. We're happy with the three. We're good." 

    She added: "He has a lot of experience with kids even though he wasn't a father, he was the oldest of 11 other kids. He's used to little kids, and he's taken it on really well and obviously loves them."
