Karlie Kloss and husband Joshua Kushner enjoy rare PDA as they share a kiss while celebrating New Ye

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 24, 2024

Karlie Kloss and husband Joshua Kushner welcomed the new year with a kiss.

The model, 31, shared a sweet video to Instagram on Wednesday, from the couple's New Year's Eve celebrations in Udaipur, India, where they traveled for a friend's wedding.

In the clip she and the businessman, 38, shared a sweet kiss as the clock struck midnight and they welcomed 2024. 

Karlie — who recently purchased fashion publication i-D — later posted a photo from the night, which saw her stun in a black gown with beaded embroidery next to Joshua, who looked handsome in a black tuxedo. 

'My forever new years date,' the mother-of-two captioned the photo, along with a white heart emoji.
