    How to Pick the Best Sun Protection Products for Your Hair?

    With various sun hair protection products available on the market, it might be confusing to find the one perfect for you. Below are the factors you can check to begin your shopping for the best UV hair protector. 

    Ingredients 

    The first thing to consider is skimming through the ingredient list. Ingredients like vitamins, antioxidants, plant extracts, essential oils, and organic compounds, including oxybenzone, provide sunscreen-like benefits and block UV radiation to safeguard hair from damage. The best hair products with sunscreen are a harmonious blend of soothing and effective ingredients that are mild on the scalp and strands and provide endless benefits from hair protection to moisturization and preventing color loss. 

    Scalp And Hair Type 

    Just like oily, acne, and sensitive skin type, there are different scalp types— oily, dry, and sensitive. Lightweight and non-greasy sun protectors for hair are perfect for oily scalp types. These balance the natural scalp oil production to prevent greasiness and clogging. If you have dry hair and scalp, opt for a moisturizing, hydrating, lightweight formula like an SPF hair spray or a conditioner that regulates scalp water levels to avert flaking and itching. A UV protection hair product with effective UV filters marked as allergy-tested, hypoallergenic, and board-certified dermatologist-tested pairs well with sensitive scalp types. 

    Formula 

    Each sun hair protection formula is different; however, the best concoctions are safe, gentle, and effective. From sunscreens to conditioners, shampoos, and sprays, the market is flooded with tons of options. You can incorporate any product into your vacation arsenal that matches your preferences. It is wise to invest in a product that features a water-resistant formula and doesn’t leave any white cast or white residue behind.

    Sunscreen Type

    When it comes to sunscreens, there are typically two types — mineral and chemical sunscreens. Mineral sunscreens are made with ingredients such as plant extracts, zinc oxide, and titanium oxide. They sit on the top of the hair strands and reflect UV rays. These are typically suitable for people with delicate hair and skin. On the other hand, chemical sunscreens for hair are developed with required concentrations of organic compounds such as oxybenzone, avobenzone, octinoxate, etc. These blends are more potent than their mineral counterparts and work by absorbing UV radiation, converting it into heat, and releasing it from the scalp and hair. You may prefer consulting a dermatologist to know which one to opt for depending on your scalp's sensitivity. 

    When in doubt, don't be afraid of experimenting with different hair sunscreen products to discover your dream formula. Additionally, consider the right approach for applying it to unlock maximum benefits.

    How to Use the Best Sun Protection Hair Products?

    Each hair sun protection product requires a different approach for application. So here we have given some easy steps to simplify the application process.

    For Sunscreen 

    1. Detangle your hair gently using a wide-toothed brush and your fingers. 

    2. Part your hair if you are using a powder-based sunscreen and massage it on the scalp. 

    3. For sprays, splash the mist evenly on hair from root to tip, followed by detangling. 

    4. Cream and lotion-based hair sunscreens can be applied the same way you use a face sunscreen. 

    For Shampoo And Conditioner 

    You can apply a shampoo with sunscreen just like a regular shampoo. For a leave-in conditioner with UV protection, take the required amount on your palms and gently slather it on strands from top to bottom. You can spray a conditioner directly if it's a mist one.

    Additionally, it's better to follow the instructions on the product label provided to ensure easy and mess-free application. Further, below are some extra tips you can follow to prevent scalp burns and frayed hair. 

    ​​​​​​​Tips to Protect Your Hair And Scalp from Sun Damage

    The best sun protection products for hair are your go-to options for quick and intense hair protection, especially when you run short of time. These products go beyond sun protection and provide additional benefits, from boosting color vibrancy to increasing manageability and strengthening hair. Besides using heat protectants, here are some extra recommendations to keep your hair healthy. 

    Hydrate Hair 

    Be sure to consume plenty of water throughout the day, especially if you travel more under the sun. This is because sun exposure and heat dry the natural hair moisture and water. Plus, it's a good idea to upgrade your diet with power-packed veggies and fruits with good water content. 

    Carry a Hat 

    While you are exploring, wear a hat to secure your scalp from direct sun exposure. Also, don't restrict yourself from removing it when you feel like it, especially when you are on a beach, enjoying in water. 

    Avoid Excess Shampooing 

    Shampooing your hair too frequently strips natural scalp oils, making the skin and hair fragile and dry. In such cases, nothing feels better than a normal or cold shower to keep your tresses clean. Also, it's not a bad thought to shampoo hair when needed. If your hair feels too oily, greasy, and dirty, try a mild and non-drying shampoo.
