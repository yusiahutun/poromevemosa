Robert Downey Jr 'gushed' over Mark Ruffalo's body after he took his shirt off

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, February 17, 2024

Robert Downey Jr is as much of a fanboy of Mark Ruffalo as we are, and the Internet loves their latest interaction.

Mark Ruffalo and Robert Downey Jr have worked together multiple times, mostly during their Avenger days. The movie stars apparently disagreed about marking their time together on set forever. Several celebrities from the Avengers cast, including Robert, Scarlett Johansson, Chris Hemsworth, and Chris Evans, decided to get identical tattoos. Mark disagreed with the plan, though and Robert wasn’t too happy about it. But it didn’t end their friendship, that’s for sure.

Robert Downey Junior thinks Mark Ruffalo’s body is pretty ‘bangable’

Hollywood heartthrobs Robert Downey Jr. and Mark Ruffalo’s praise each other in their new Variety interview. Robert in particular talked about Mark’s outstanding performance in Poor Things where the Hulk actor had to ‘bare it all’ for the camera.

Speaking of the risky performance and Mark’s amazing body, Robert said: “But I’ve got to say, you look pretty bangable to me, in case you were wondering.”

Ruffalo takes the compliment with a wide smile adding he had to wear an “a– pad” for the character. He explains the director Yorgos Lanthimos “really wanted the silhouette,” so he had to wear a corset, a shoulder pad, leg pads, and more.

“Mark blushes at Downey’s compliments”

Marvel fans are swooning over Robert fanboying over his former co-star.

One commented: “Mark blushing at Downey’s compliments and praise is adorable.”

Another fan commented on YouTube: “It is so wholesome to see the genuine appreciation and friendship RDJ and Mark had for each other.”

“The deep respect they have for each other, this extremely honest conversation is inspiring, warm and absolutely stellar. This side of Downey is not something we get on camera often; Whoever did this pairing was absolutely on point (totally helps that I’ve such a huge crush on Ruffalo),” said a third fan.
