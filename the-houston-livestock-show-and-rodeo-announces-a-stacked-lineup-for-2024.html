The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo Announces A Stacked Lineup For 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 2, 2024

HOUSTON, TX (CelebrityAccess) — The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo revealed a diverse lineup for the 2024 rodeo season, which lands in Houston from Feb. 27 – March 17.

“The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo is proud to bring such a wide range of musical genres to the RODEOHOUSTON stage,” said Chris Boleman, Rodeo president and CEO. “We’re welcoming 10 new entertainers who will make their debut on the star stage, as well as many fan-favorites from a variety of genres from our country roots, to EDM and hip-hop.”

The diverse array of performers set to take the stage at Houston’s NRG Stadium includes country favorites such as Luke Bryan, Blake Shelton, and Hank Williams Jr., to hip-hop icons such as 50 Cent and EDM star Major Lazer.

The concert series will also see performances from For King + Country, Los Tigres del Norte, Bun B, Zac Brown Band, Brad Paisley, and Whiskey Myers.

Newcomers to the rodeo for 2024 include Carly Pearce, Ivan Cornejo, Nickelback, Oliver Anthony, Jelly Roll, and Lainey Wilson, among others.

Eric Church is slated to close the rodeo’s live performances when he takes the stage on Sunday, March 17th.
