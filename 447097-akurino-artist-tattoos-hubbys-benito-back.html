Akorino Makeup Artist Carey Priscilla Tattoos Hubbys Name on Her Back on Son's Birthday

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 17, 2024
  • Carey Priscilla has been married to Benito Muriu for two years now, and they have a son together
  • The Akorino model and makeup artist has tattooed her husband's name, and she is proud of it as she showcased it
  • The content creators keep unlocking new levels to their love, and their fans love seeing it

Akorino makeup artist Carey Priscilla has tattooed her husband's name 'Benito' on her back and notes that he always "got her back".

The mom of one took to her Instagram page to post videos of her tattooing process on Instagram and she captioned it:

"Surprising my husband with his name tattooed on my back during our son's first birthday I love you babe! thank you for buying me my dream car.

The grand reveal

She uploaded two videos to her Instagram each had different wordings on the videos.

Carey Priscilla prayed for husband at 16

TUKO.co.ke had previously reported that the Akorino model and makeup artist said that she started praying for a husband before turning 18.

"I started praying for what I wanted in a husband when I was about sixteen! This is after I realized I would love to build a home with somebody’s son in future.," Carey said.

The mother of one said she prayed for all the qualities she wanted in a spouse, a good character and fine looks.

"It may sound far-fetched but I see the results every day in my marriage. I believe God granted me my heart’s desire according to His Will when I finally met my husband," she added.
