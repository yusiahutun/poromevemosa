19 Best Retinol Cream Moisturizers That'll Save Your Skin

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

Turning back the clock isn’t an option, so when you want healthier, glowing skin, turning to products with proven results is the best way to go. If there’s one beauty item that has consistently been touted as a game-changer in the skincare industry, it’s retinol cream moisturizer. Regardless of your age or skin type, chances are you’ve used a form of retinol cream to combat a skin issue. But why is that? What makes it so powerful? In fact, what is retinol?

What is retinol cream moisturizer — and what can it do for you?

Retinol is a type of retinoid, which is a derivative of vitamin A. Due to its extremely small molecular structure, retinol can penetrate deep into the skin where it unclogs pores, smooths wrinkles, improves skin’s overall texture, and boosts collagen, thanks to vitamin A’s natural ability to improve skin elasticity and moisture. This is why retinol has been used for decades to do everything from treating skin conditions like acne, hyperpigmentation, and psoriasis, to minimizing the look of fine lines and wrinkles in mature skin.

While retinol is easily one of the most popular skincare ingredients, not all retinol creams are created equal. The concentration of retinol found in over-the-counter retinol creams is significantly lower than that in prescription creams like Retin-A. There is a good reason for the different concentrations. Due to its potency, retinol amounts must be monitored. Over-the-counter products are made with an amount that has been deemed safe for most skin types. This gives you the opportunity to either save a trip to your dermatologist or figure out that you need a prescription cream with a higher concentration of retinol.
