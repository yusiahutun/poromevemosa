Mario Lopez Admits He Sleeps Naked, Reveals His &quot;Sweet&quot; Celebrity Crush

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 1, 2024

Mario Lopez is airing his dirty laundry!

The "Saved By the Bell" alum stops by "The Real's" pajama party on Friday, where he reveals his biggest celebrity crush, his guilty pleasure and details on his new memoir, "Just Between Us."

toofab's Rachel Hoffman had a front row seat at the slumber party -- and we're giving you some extra dirty details that might not be on the show.

"I think every guy growing up had a crush on Alyssa Milano from 'Who's the Boss,'" Mario admits. "She's so sweet. I tell her every time I see her to interview her."

The ladies of "The Real" also get real about their childhood puppy love!

Tamara Mowry admits that she used to have a thing for "The Sandlot's" Mike Vitar (see him now here), Tamar Braxton says has always crushed on L.L. Cool J and Jeannie Mai used to dream about Jordan McKnight.

The most surprising crush? Loni Love used to love Sammy Davis, Jr!

Adrienne Bailon comes clean to Mario, admitting that his "Saved By the Bell" character A.C. Slater caught her eye.

The 40-year-old actor also talks about his guilty pleasure -- and that's when things got a little dirty! "Whenever I'm getting any type of pleasure, I don't feel guilty about it," he says with a smile.

And while Lopez was rocking his favorite red plaid pajamas, he prefers to be in the buff when he sleeps. "I don't even sleep in pajamas. I go nature boy when I sleep," he explains.

Check out the clip below to hear more from Mario!

The juiciest moment of the show comes during a classic game of Truth or Dare. The girls must answer an interesting question or complete a crazy dare. Let's just say things get REAL when Adrienne is dared to eat a chocolate-covered cricket!
