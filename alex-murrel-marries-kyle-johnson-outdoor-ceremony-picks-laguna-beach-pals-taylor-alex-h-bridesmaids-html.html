Alex Murrel marries Kyle Johnson with Laguna Beach's Taylor and Alex H

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Alex Murrel married fiancé Kyle Johnson in a picturesque ceremony in Malibu, California on Saturday.

The bride, a season 2 cast member on Laguna Beach, chose two of her former castmates to act as her bridesmaids at the ceremony at the Calamigos Ranch.

The maids, Taylor Cole and Alex Hooser, posted a photo booth collage of Alex and the rest of the ladies, who wore maroon, floor-length gowns.
