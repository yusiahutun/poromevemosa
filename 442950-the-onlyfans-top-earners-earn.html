How does OnlyFans work?

To access the content, fans pay a monthly subscription fee set by the creator. The cost typically ranges from a few dollars to several hundred dollars per month.

Creators can also offer additional paid content, such as custom videos or photos, for an additional fee. OnlyFans takes a percentage of the earnings, typically around 20%, while the rest goes to the creator.
