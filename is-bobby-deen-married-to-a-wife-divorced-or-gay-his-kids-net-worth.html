American chef Bobby Deen is the dashing son of Paula Deen, who is also a popular cook and television personality. However, as the family business expanded and became more sophisticated, the two brothers left the show to focus on the family restaurant.

After several years, Bobby Deen returned to the airwaves with his personal cooking show, Not My Mama’s Meals, which began in January 2012. His new cooking show was not only about cooking and eating, but he reworked his mother’s recipes into healthier recipes.
