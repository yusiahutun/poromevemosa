Jennifer Garner Offers Homeless Man in Wheelchair Her Shoes, Puts Socks on His Feet

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 6, 2024
  • Jennifer Garner and a paparazzo joined forces to help a homeless man in a touching act of compassion
  • Garner handed the homeless man a plastic bag filled with essential items, and provided socks; she also attempted to give him her sneakers
  • The photographer, touched by the actress' kindness, contributed by graciously offering his sneakers

Hollywood actress Jennifer Garner has shown her compassionate side.

Garner joined forces with a paparazzo to help a homeless man in need.

According to Page Six, the heartwarming incident unfolded in a Santa Monica Beach parking lot, where Garner, 51, was being filmed by the photographer.

How did Jennifer Garner meet homeless man?

As the paparazzo was documenting Garner's activities, she spotted a homeless man in a wheelchair and decided to make a difference.

The celebrity engaged in conversation with the man, who was wearing a green T-shirt and handed him a plastic bag filled with essential items.

Upon noticing that the man was barefoot, she took swift action.

She opened her car door, stepped out, crouched in front of him, and began putting socks on his feet.

Garner even attempted to give the man her own sneakers, but they proved to be too small.

Undeterred, she turned to the paparazzo, who was a few feet away, and asked;

"What size feet do you have? Can I buy your shoes for him? He needs a shoe."

Kind photographer

The photographer offered his size-11 sneakers, declining any payment from the Golden Globe-winning actress.

Garner graciously accepted the shoes and placed them on the grateful man's feet.

To further assist the homeless individual, Garner shared that she had a blanket in her car and offered it to him.

Homeless man not ready to return home

Separately, a man currently living in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) got many people feeling sad after he opened up about the bad conditions that he finds himself in.

In a since-deleted TikTok video, the middle-aged Ghanaian man who spoke about his plight in Dubai admitted that things are tough for him and his other Ghanaian colleagues living in the country.

He revealed that he is homeless in Dubai and sleeps behind a flowerpot at the bus station along with some of his Ghanaian friends.

His reason was that the standard of living in Ghana had not improved; hence would prefer to stay on.

"Things are not better in Ghana, so for me to return and be disgraced, I would prefer to stay here," he said with confidence.
