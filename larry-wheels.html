Biography TimeLine

Larry Wheels is an American powerlifter, bodybuilder, and social media influencer. Here is a detailed biography timeline of Larry Wheels:

  • 2011: At 17, Wheels began weight training to improve his performance as a high school football player.

  • 2021: Wheels won the 2021 Arnold Classic Australia Pro Raw Challenge in the 110kg weight class, setting a world record with a total lift of 2,338 pounds.
