Gwyneth Paltrows son Moses looks just like dad Chris Martin in rare birthday pic

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

Gwyneth was recently supported by her son and daughter Apple during a Utah ski trial

    Gwyneth Paltrow has paid a heartfelt tribute to "the boy that fills my soul up every time I look at him," her rarely-seen son Moses on his 17th birthday. 

    The actress - who was recently supported by her son and daughter Apple during a Utah ski trial - took to social media to honor her "exceptional, kind, loving" son and fans couldn't believe how much he looked like his father, Chris Martin.

    "Happy 17th birthday to the boy that fills my soul up every time I look at him. @mosesmartin you are the most exceptional, kind, loving human being," Gwyneth posted alongside a selfie of the pair. "You keep us all laughing with your perfect impressions and you inspire us with your harmonies. I deeply adore you more than you could ever imagine! Love, mama."

    "He looks just like his father," commented one fan as another added: "Happy Birthday Moses! He looks just like his dad!" 

    Gwyneth and Chris, the frontman of Coldplay, met in 2022 and marred in December 2003 in a ceremony at a hotel in Southern California. She was pregnant with their daughter Apple at the time, and they later welcomed Moses in 2006. 

    They shocked fans in 2014 when they announced they had "consciously uncoupled," and their divorce was finalized in 2016.
