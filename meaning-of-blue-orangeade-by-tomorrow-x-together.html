Meaning of Blue Orangeade by TOMORROW X TOGETHER

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, February 25, 2024

The song "Blue Orangeade" by TOMORROW X TOGETHER is a vibrant and catchy track that explores the concept of complementary colors and how different elements can come together to create something beautiful and unique. The overall theme of the song revolves around the idea of opposites attracting and how embracing our differences can lead to a more colorful and fulfilling life.One standout lyric in the song is "넌 빨간 장미를 좋아해, 넌 파란 바다를 좋아해" (You like red roses, you like the blue sea). These contrasting preferences reflect the different tastes and personalities of two individuals. The lyric suggests that despite having different preferences, these differences can contribute to a more exciting and diverse relationship. It develops the theme of embracing individuality and appreciating the beauty that can be found in contrasting elements.Another key lyric is "우린 정반대인 거야, 그래서 더 특별한 거야" (We are opposites, that's why we are more special). This lyric emphasizes the uniqueness and specialness that comes from being opposites. It suggests that the relationship between two people with contrasting qualities can be more interesting and memorable. It further develops the theme of celebrating differences and finding beauty in the unexpected.The lyrics "넌 강아지, 난 kitty kitty (You), 우린 S, 또 N 자석 같아, 네 옆에 존재해야 완벽한걸" (You are a puppy, I'm kitty kitty, We're like magnets, we have to exist next to each other to be perfect) also contribute to the overall theme of opposites attracting. Comparing themselves to different animals and describing their connection as magnetic highlights the strong attraction they have for each other despite their differences. This lyric reinforces the idea that opposites can complement and complete each other, enriching their relationship.Additionally, the lyric "컴컴한 세상 그 속에, 넌 내 제일 멋진 색인걸" (In a dark world, you are my coolest color) adds a layer of depth to the theme. It suggests that in a world full of darkness and negativity, the presence of the other person brings light and joy, making them the most vibrant and important color in their life. This lyric showcases the transformative power of love and how it can brighten one's perspective, even in the darkest times.Lastly, the lyric "Minus one plus plus one, I can stay young cause of you" (Minus one plus plus one, I can stay young because of you) introduces a theme of rejuvenation and vitality. The idea that being with someone who brings out the best in you can help you retain a youthful energy and spirit. It emphasizes the positive impact that two contrasting elements can have on each other, inspiring growth and keeping the relationship dynamic.Overall, the song "Blue Orangeade" by TOMORROW X TOGETHER is an expression of the beauty and importance of embracing our differences. It celebrates the concept of opposites attracting and highlights the transformative power of love and acceptance. Through its imaginative and creative lyrics, the song urges listeners to appreciate and find harmony in the colorful kaleidoscope of life, just like the blending of complementary colors.
