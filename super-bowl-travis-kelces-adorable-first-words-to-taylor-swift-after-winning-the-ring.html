Super Bowl: Travis Kelce's adorable first words to Taylor Swift after winning the ring

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, April 15, 2024

Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs have gone and done it, and won their fourth Super Bowl title! Of course, the celebrations with Taylor Swift were the photos all the Swifties were waiting for, and the moment they locked eyes after the big win, and their first words to each other were caught on camera.

Fans went wild last night as Travis Kelce and the team beat the San Francisco 49ers as Taylor Swift, who had just flown over from back-to-back shows from Tokyo watched on in the crowd. As the final whistle sounded, Swifties across the globe knew they had what they were waiting for all along. A cute PDA moment from their favorite celebrity couple, and they did not disappoint.

Travis Kelce’s first words to Taylor Swift after winning the Super Bowl

After the epic 25-22 win from the Chiefs, the players’ family and friends came onto the pitch to celebrate, including Donna Kelce, and Taylor Swift.

As they came down from the stands, the music star sweetly waited behind her (hopefully) future mother-in-law, to let her have the first hug with her son, which many social media users were calling ‘sweet’.

Then, the big moment came as Travis and Taylor met for the first time since the big title win. With seas of applause and photographers around them, the NFL star went to hug Taylor as he said “Come here girl,” followed by the cute PDA moment we were all waiting for.

The couple then proceeded to share a kiss, similar to that of the Baltimore Ravens game. Yep, you guessed it, the crowd (and Twitter) went wild.

“I can’t believe that Travis Kelce made a friendship bracelet for Taylor Swift and now we are yelling at our TVs,” wrote one fan.

“AMERICA’S SWEETHEARTS!!” exclaimed a second.

A third penned: “She flew across the world after performing 4 sold out shows to watch her man win the Super Bowl…. this is the best rom-com I’ve ever seen in my life,” honestly, same.

The star celebrated with her friends after the epic win

Before heading down to celebrate with her boo, as soon as the final whistle blew, Swift and her celebrity friends, including Ice Spice, Lana Del Rey and Blake Lively.

The group could be seen ecstatically jumping around and celebrating, with the Summertime Sadness singer even losing her balance at one point!

Other celeb appearances included Justin Bieber and his wife Hailey, although unfortunately for some fans, he remained in the stands.

Rumors were flying around that the Baby singer had flown to Vegas to join Usher in his Super Bowl half-time performance, but unfortunately, that wasn’t the case. Nonetheless, Usher still put on an epic show!
