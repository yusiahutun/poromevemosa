Posh swings into action on the set of her dress collection photo shoot

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

    With a wealth of experience in front of the camera, Armani poster girl Victoria Beckham was the perfect person to show a group of models how it's done at a photo session on Thursday. 

    Immaculate in a dress of her own design – a £2,095 cashmere-blend number – killer Christian Louboutin heels, and with her trademark sunglasses and pout in place, Posh personally directed the shoot for her autumn/winter 2009 range. 

    At one point the fashionista was seen apparently demonstrating the pose she wanted by perching on a swing that was one of the props. Then the mum-of-three got behind the camera, giving instructions as a number of young models wearing her latest pieces swung on the porches of Georgian townhouses. 

    It is the second collection of capsule dresses from the 35-year-old – who jetted into London from her LA base on Tuesday to prepare for the release of her new designs - after launching her label to the excitement of fans in the British capital in December last year. "I feel like I'm competing in an arena where I belong," she said at the time. "I was so nervous because this has been a lifetime in the making."
