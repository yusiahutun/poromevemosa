Kate exudes country cool at wedding of rock pal Bobby

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 12, 2024

    With Kate Moss as one of your wedding guests there's always a chance you could be overshadowed, but Primal Scream frontman Bobby Gillespie and his bride neatly sidestepped the problem by arriving in rather flamboyant outfits themselves. At the bohemian rustic-themed celebrations this weekend his sweetheart, stylist Katy England wore a pink and white candy-striped gown.

    The rock wildman was no less eye-catching in a white suit embroidered with roses. Attending the couple at the service in St Margaret's Church Staffordshire were their sons, four-year-old Wolf and Lux, who's nearly two.

    Never one to fade into the background, Kate had chosen a striking look for her friends' special day. The glamorous covergirl arrived to give a reading in pale blue shorts and a matching jacket with her former partner Jefferson Hack and their daughter Lila Grace. Sadie Frost, Jade Jagger and Mick Jones of The Clash also joined the roll call of starry invitees who witnessed the couple's exchange of vows.

    Over in France some equally romantic nuptials took place when The Da Vinci Code actor Jean Reno wed Zofia Borucka in the presence of the country's iconic singer Johnny Hallyday. His model bride will have taken the 58-year-old star's breath away when she made her appearance in an exquisite ivory chiffon dress, with flowers adorning her brunette locks.
