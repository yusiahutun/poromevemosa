Save 30% on Sydney Sweeney's Go-To Lip Mask

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

As seen in her Go to Bed With Me video, Euphoria and White Lotus star Sydney Sweeney swears by a number of tried-and-true beauty products, but Laneige's buzzy Lip Sleeping Mask is one holy-grail favorite she's relied on since college for supremely supple lips.

"Once I got to college, I started realizing that it was not a good idea to wash my face with just water and zero products," she previously told Bazaar. "I started using the Laneige Lip Sleeping Mask, and I became obsessed. I tried the brand's other products and started incorporating them into my skin care routine little by little, and then almost all of my products became Laneige."
