Lunar Empress Qiyana Skin: Splash Art, Price &amp; Release Date

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, January 27, 2024

Riot has officially revealed Lunar Empress Qiyana skin, scheduled to release on patch 13.1.

The Empress of the Elements, Qiyana, was released about three years ago into League of Legends. Her ability to catch someone out, be flexible and land combos make her appealing to players. Thus, she has been one of the more popular assassins in League of Legends.

Since her release, she has had five skin releases so far. The skins are Battle Boss Qiyana, True Damage Qiyana, Battle Queen Qiyana, Shockblade Qiyana, and Prestige True Damage Qiyana. All the skins have something nice to offer, as almost all of them have chromas.

With the new Lunar update coming early next year, a new skin for Qiyana has been revealed. The Lunar 2023 skin line will consist of skins for Ashe, Khaâ€™Zix, Thresh, and Malphite alongside Qiyana.

The update will also have another skin line coming to the game called Mythmakers. This skin line will feature skins for Irelia, Garen, Galio, Sivir, and Zyra. Together, both the new skin lines have been released in PBE.

So, Qiyana will be getting another skin at the start of the next year. There was a video released for the skin in action along with the Chromas that are coming for the skin. Here is what we know so far.
