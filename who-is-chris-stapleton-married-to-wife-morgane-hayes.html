Chris Stapletons Wife Morgane Is a Talented Performer! Meet the Singers Longtime Spouse

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Chris Stapleton’s wife, Morgane Stapleton, is a natural-born talent! The country superstar is often joined on stage by his beautiful spouse to perform incredible music together.

Who Is Morgane Stapleton?

Just like Chris, Morgane is an experienced singer-songwriter. The Nashville native first met Chris in 2003 as they were both looking to kickstart their careers in the country music genre. On their first date, they sweetly wrote a song together.

The lovebirds got married in 2007 and have been living happily together ever since. After more than 15 years together, the “Tennessee Whiskey” singer and his beloved shared the secret to their lasting marriage.

“The key to a successful marriage, I’d say communication,” he told Fox News Digital at the 2023 Rock & Roll Hall of Fame induction ceremony in November 2023. “We’re always working on that … love and kindness and patience. I think you have those things in play, hopefully, you can overcome whatever obstacles.”

Fans were elated to see Chris and Morgane attend the 2023 CMA Awards together in November 2023. She dazzled in a black gown with colorful floral accents, while her husband wore a black suit and his signature cowboy hat.

Do Chris and Morgane Stapleton Have Any Kids?

The pair are parents to five children. In 2009, the pair welcomed their first child together, son Waylon. Their second child, daughter Ada, was born in 2010. Eight years later, Chris and Morgane welcomed their twins, Macon and Samuel. Their birth was announced by Reba McEntire at the 53rd Academy of Country Music Awards, explaining why the hitmaker missed out on the ceremony. “Those little rascals came a little bit early,” she told the audience at the time.

In 2019, the couple welcomed their youngest child. They have yet to reveal the little one’s name to the public. Chris and Morgane reflected on their baby’s arrival in an Instagram post.

“Sweet boy … It’s hard to believe almost a month has already gone by,” the doting mom of five captioned a photo on May 29, 2019. “Time flies faster than I ever thought imaginable. My heart is so full!”

For the most part, the songwriters have been raising their kids out of the spotlight, only making a few rare public appearances as a family at events over the years.

“I signed up for this, my children did not,” Chris told The Boot in 2018. “I am a very private person, and in that respect, I keep my children out of it. They’re children, and they deserve the chance to be children.”

During the 2018 Country Music Association Awards, Chris gave his kids a special shout-out in his acceptance speech.

“Waylon and Ada, if you’re out there tonight, and the twin boys, and the one on the way, I love you so much,” he said.
