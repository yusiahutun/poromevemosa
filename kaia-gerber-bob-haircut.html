Kaia Gerber's new bob - Short haircut

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Another week, another A-lister boards the bob brigade. This time Kaia Gerber revealed a statement haircut, showcasing her short style on Instagram, which she coined “the drama cut”.

The supermodel’s new ‘do was courtesy of legendary hairstylist Guido Palau, the global creative director for Redken, and his first assistant, Sandy Hullett, who is also a master session stylist. Clearly one pro is not enough for a haircut this impossibly chic.

Gerber is the latest in a line of famous faces to go for the chop, with actresses Reese Witherspoon, Lily James and Natalie Portman all debuting bobs in the past few weeks.

Whether jaw-length like Gerber’s and James’s, shoulder-grazing like Witherspoon’s, or somewhere in between like Portman’s it’s clear that the bob is the cut of the summer, however you wear it.
