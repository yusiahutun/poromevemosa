9 Best Black-Owned Subscription Boxes to Shop in 2022: Glory Skincare, Curlbox, More

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

Created by a former librarian to celebrate and share fictional and non-fictional works exclusively by Black authors, the Call Number subscription box is a quarterly service that's ideal for any serious book lover. Subscribers can choose between Call Number's book-only or complete subscription, which features a newly released book with up to three library- or book-related extras, like a catalog card, spine label, label protector, or logo sticker.

Cost: Ranges from $40 to $156 for a complete quarterly fiction, non-fiction, or indie subscription, and $23 to $88 for a book-only quarterly fiction, non-fiction, indie, or Young Adult subscription.
