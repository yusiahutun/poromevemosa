Three Children With Ex-Husband

Anansa and her ex-husband, David Patterson's romantic history started way back in 2008. After dating for around two years, the couple decided to get married.

They exchanged their wedding vows on 10 July 2010 at First Congregational Church, Columbus, Ohio.

Anansa Sims with her ex-husband, David Patterson, during their wedding (Photo: essence.com)

In the course of their married life, they welcomed three children. Their firstborn was a daughter named Ava Brielle Patterson (6 July 2011).

Likewise, their two sons David Bernard Patterson III was born on 6 November 2012 and Dean Danny Benjamin Patterson on 20 June 2014. Dean graduated from pre-school in July 2019.
