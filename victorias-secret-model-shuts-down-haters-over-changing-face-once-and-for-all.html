Victoria's Secret model shuts down haters over 'changing face' once and for all

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Adriana Lima has ‘clapped back’ at haters who suggested she underwent cosmetic surgery, including a facelift, after her appearance at the Hunger Games premiere.

The former Victoria’s Secret Model, who gave birth a year ago, attended the movie premiere in LA this week, and fans were saying she looked ‘unrecognizable.’

Adriana Lima ‘claps back’ at haters claiming she had work done

The comments didn’t go unnoticed by the 42-year-old, who took to her Instagram story to shut the haters down once and for all.

Posting a no-make-up selfie onto her story, the star wrote: “The face of a tired mom of one teenage girl, two pre-teens, one active boy, a 1-year-old learning to walk and three dogs… thanks for your concern.”

Lima gave birth to her third child, son Cyan Lima in 2022, her first with her current partner, Andre Lemmers. She also has two daughters, Sienna, 11, and Valentina, 14, whom she shares with her ex-husband Marko Jarić.

After her revelation, fans rushed to X, formerly known as Twitter, to praise the new mother for talking about the changes to her face/body.

“Adriana’s openness is inspiring and empowering for other women. It shows that even someone who is considered to be ‘perfect’ by society’s standards can struggle with body image and acceptance,” wrote one.

Another penned: “She is beautiful, always has been and continues to be. The truth is that a lot of people criticize weight change. They expect her, at 42, to have the same face and body she had at 19.”

“You have to be really stupid to criticize Adriana Lima’s face,” one fan wrote.

The model opens up about ’embracing’ her postpartum body

Earlier this year, Adriana opened up about how she struggles with her postpartum body every day, but was learning to embrace the change.

“Every day I have to remind myself that, ‘Listen, accept who you are. Accept your body,'” she told PEOPLE.

“Every day, I have to remind myself, I’m human. I get insecure here and there.” She then explained how, “with age, your body reacts differently. But it’s okay. I’m learning.”

The Brazilian model began working with Victoria’s Secret in 1999 -2018, starting her career with the giant when she was 18 years old.
