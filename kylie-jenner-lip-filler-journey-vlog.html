Kylie Jenner Is Releasing a Vlog All About Her Lip Filler Journey

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 12, 2024

Ever since Kylie Jenner removed her infamous lip fillers, fans have been speculating about why she did it. Was it because of Stormi? Will she ever get injections again? The makeup mogul has kept quiet on the topic since breaking the news casually in an Instagram comment, but she'll soon be releasing an entire video on the topic.

Late last night, Jenner took to Twitter to update fans on her life. Her first few Tweets were about her latest Kylie Cosmetics launches and about Stormi. A fan asked over Twitter about the vlog Jenner's been working on for a while, and that's when she let it spill what the topic of the video is about.

"The vlog is just a whole mess & I’ve been the busiest I’ve everrrr been," she wrote. But the most shocking revelation came next: "I’m editing myself." Jenner—who is about to be worth a cool billion dollars and has been on television for over a decade—is editing her own vlogs. She continued, "It’s about my whole lip filler journey. I hope it comes out soon."

On July 30, Jenner tweeted that the vlog is "taking way longer than I thought. But I’m aiming for this week!" She first let fans know that she filmed her first vlog on July 11—but didn't reveal the topic.

The last Youtube video Jenner uploaded was a month ago. It was a Q+A filmed with her best friend Jordyn Woods, who she'll soon be releasing a Kylie Cosmetics collaboration with.
