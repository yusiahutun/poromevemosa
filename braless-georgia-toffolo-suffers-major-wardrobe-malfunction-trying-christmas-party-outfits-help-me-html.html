Braless Georgia Toffolo suffers MAJOR wardrobe malfunction while trying on Christmas party outfits:

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Georgia Toffolo suffered a wardrobe malfunction while trying on outfits for her Christmas party on Thursday.

The reality star, 29, was gearing up to host a festive do at her home but was struggling to decide what to wear and turned to her 1.7 million Instagram followers for help.

The former Made in Chelsea star tried on a host of glamorous outfits but had just 20 minutes to decide until guests started to arrive.

When trying  on a pair of trousers she had bought recently, Georgia was almost caught short as she tried on a sleek black suit and struggled to get them to fasten. 

She said: 'These trousers - oh my God - won't do up - and I only tried them on about three weeks ago. But that's the beauty of this outfit - no one will know. What do we think?'

Georgia Toffolo suffered a major wardrobe malfunction while trying on outfits for her Christmas party on Thursday as she suffered a nip slip and couldn't do up her trousers 

Toff got herself in a 'tizz' while trying to select a suitable outfit for her Christmas party with just minutes to spare 

The former I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! winner began tugging on the zip in desperation but ended up exposing herself as she suffered a nip slip while going braless under the blazer. 

As she shrieked 'oh my god!' Toff rushed to protect her modesty but eventually decided that the pants and suit combo was ' too much'.

Georgia explained to her followers: 'Help me! Everyone is having a nice time and I'm freaking out - I don't know what to wear.

'I've been desperate to wear this bubble dress but I think it's just a little bit OTT.

'I just don't think I can wear this, I've really got myself in a tizz!'

While Georgia was almost convinced that the outfit was the right choice, she decided to try something else when she realised that the bottom of the dress was almost far too short. 

The TV star was 'obsessed' with her next choice of a long glittering dress but eventually decided that it was 'too intense' for her party, especially when paired with a matching handbag.

The best-selling author asked if the number was 'too much' and lamented to her followers that she had got herself so 'stressed' over the situation.

The Meet Me... author gave followers a glimpse of her leg in a shimmering festive number but eventually decided that it was too over the top

Georgia wondered if this dress was a little bit too much after noticing just how short it was 

In the end, Georgia decided on a minidress complete with a pair of thigh-high boots to cover all bases. 

She said: 'I've got, literally a minute, I think I've nailed it. 

'This cute little Valentino number with my Russel and Bromley boots so I can host well.

'Even if I can do some lady dancing, no-one can see my knickers! See you later!'
