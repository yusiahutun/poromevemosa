Kelly Ripa and Mark Consuelos' son Michael celebrates 26th birthday with rare social media post: 'Is

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

NEW YORK CITY, NEW YORK: ‘Live with Kelly and Mark’ hosts Kelly Ripa and Mark Consuelos's son Michael Consuelos celebrates his 26th birthday. The couple share three children, two sons, Michael and Joaquin, and a daughter, Lola. Michael is the oldest of Kelly and Mark’s three kids.

Recently, Kelly and Mark’s oldest son, Michael celebrated his 26th birthday and shared an Instagram story that reflected a sarcastic moment of "self-doubt." The ‘Live’ host and couple have a strong and loving relationship with their son. He has grown up in the spotlight owing to his parent’s fame in the entertainment industry. Moreover, the ‘Riverdale’ actor has often expressed his pride in his son’s accomplishments and has been actively involved in his life. On the other hand, Kelly nurtures Michael’s talents and they’re often seen together at public events. Mark frequently shares heartwarming posts on social media with his children.

Michael Consuelos' self-doubt question

‘Live’ hosts Kelly Ripa and Mark Consuelos’s dashing son Michael has celebrated his birthday by sharing an intimate photo with an existential question. He shared his photo on his Instagram story with the caption, “Is 26 pushing 30?” He looked sleek in a chic minimalist grey crewneck sweatshirt and a smile. Michael looked like a younger version of Mark as he wore big glasses frames and gave a boyish outlook of two attractive folks with his brunette hair pushed back stylishly. He held a mug that read, “Out of Office” to match his casual fit. His caption about his age on Instagram Story worried him, which can be seen in his stressed yet smiley photo.

Mark and Kelly’s following superstar

Michael Consuelos was born on June 2, 1997, in New York City. He attended New York University from 2016 to 2020 and is currently an actor, director, and writer who appeared in multiple films and TV shows. As per his IMDb page, he has starred in the shows namely, ‘Duck Dodgers’, ‘Riverdale’, and ‘Go, Diego Go!’ Other than this, he appeared in a short, ‘The Bensonhurst Spelling Bee’ and in the 2022 movie ‘Let’s Get Physical’. He is next in line to Mark and Kelly to become a rising star as he has a large social media presence with over 81K followers on his Instagram page. Kelly revealed to People, “he has the strongest moral compass of anyone I’ve ever known. He is just such a good and decent and thoroughly kind person”. Michael has two siblings, a younger brother Joaquin and a 21-year-old sister, Lola who is pursuing a music career.
