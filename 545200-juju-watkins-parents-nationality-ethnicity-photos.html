Juju Watkins' early life

The basketball player was born in Los Angeles, California, on July 15, 2005. She is the youngest of Robert and Sari Watkins' four children. Does Juju Watkins have any siblings? Yes. Her siblings are Dami, Mali, and Mar Watkins, but little is known about them.

Who is Juju Watkins' father?

Her father is Robert Neal-Watkins, a resident of Los Angeles, California, United States. He is American in nationality and African-American in ethnicity.

Juju Watkins' father coached her in basketball in their backyard. When she was seven, she used to play basketball in a park. She trained with Phil Handy, an assistant coach for the Los Angeles Lakers.

Robert Neal Watkins is the son of Theodore Watkins, also known as Ted Watkins Sr. He is American and of African-American ethnicity. In 1965, he started the Watts Labor Community Action Committee (WLCAC) to make life better for people living in Watts.

In 2021, she amassed 44 points. Additionally, in 2022, she was selected to represent the Women's U17 National Team of USA Basketball. With the U16 and U17 Women's National Teams, she represented the United States at the 2021 and 2022 FIBA World Cups.
