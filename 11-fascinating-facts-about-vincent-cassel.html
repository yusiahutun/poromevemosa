Vincent Cassel is of French and Brazilian descent.

Vincent Cassel, born on November 23, 1966, in Paris, France, is the son of French actor Jean-Pierre Cassel and Brazilian actress Cilene Nunes. His multicultural background has influenced his versatile acting career.

He made his breakthrough in the film “La Haine” (1995).

In the critically acclaimed French movie “La Haine,” Cassel played the role of Vinz, a troubled youth living in the outskirts of Paris. The film’s gritty realism and Cassel’s powerful performance catapulted him into the international spotlight.

Vincent Cassel is fluent in multiple languages.

With his diverse heritage and international upbringing, Cassel is fluent in French, English, Portuguese, Italian, and basic Russian. His linguistic skills have allowed him to work on various projects across different countries.

He portrayed the infamous serial killer Jacques Mesrine.

In the biographical crime film “Mesrine: Killer Instinct” (2008) and its sequel “Mesrine: Public Enemy #1” (2008), Cassel gave a chilling and captivating performance as the notorious French criminal Jacques Mesrine.

Cassel starred alongside Natalie Portman in “Black Swan” (2010).

In Darren Aronofsky’s psychological thriller “Black Swan,” Cassel portrayed Thomas Leroy, the manipulative and demanding ballet director. His dynamic performance earned him critical acclaim and further established his international reputation.

He has collaborated multiple times with director Gaspar Noé.

Vincent Cassel has worked with the controversial Argentine-French filmmaker Gaspar Noé on films like “Irréversible” (2002) and “Enter the Void” (2009). These collaborations have showcased Cassel’s willingness to take on challenging and provocative roles.

Cassel played a major role in the “Ocean’s” trilogy.

Cassel joined an ensemble cast in Steven Soderbergh’s popular heist film trilogy “Ocean’s Twelve” (2004), “Ocean’s Thirteen” (2007), and “Ocean’s Eight” (2018). His character, François Toulour, added an extra layer of excitement to the already star-studded films.

Vincent Cassel has a passion for photography.

Aside from his acting career, Cassel is an avid photographer. He often captures candid moments behind the scenes of his film projects and has exhibited his work in several art galleries.

He married fellow actress Monica Bellucci in 1999.

Cassel and Bellucci met on the set of the 1996 film “The Apartment” and got married three years later. They appeared together in multiple films and became one of the industry’s most glamorous and beloved couples. However, they announced their separation in 2013.

Cassel has two daughters.

From his marriage to Monica Bellucci, Vincent Cassel has two daughters named Deva and Léonie. Both girls have also been involved in the entertainment industry, following in their parents’ footsteps.

Vincent Cassel has a wide range of film credits.

Throughout his career, Cassel has starred in a variety of genres, including crime dramas, romances, comedies, and historical films. His versatility as an actor has allowed him to collaborate with renowned directors from around the world.
