Olivia Palermo cuts an elegant figure at a celebration of PAIGE's new collection

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 9, 2024

Olivia Palermo cut an elegant figure as she stepped out at a celebration of the PAIGE spring collection on Wednesday evening.

The American socialite, 37, looked effortlessly glamorous as she led the star-studded guest list at the festivities held at London's The Twenty Two.

She put on a glowing display in a floating black satin gown from the brand, which she styled with an extravagant feathered jacket.

Olivia, who was joined at the affair by her husband Johannes Huebl, gave herself a few extra inches in a pair of pointed black heeled boots.

She kept her essentials in a simple black clutch bag and let her outfit do all the talking as she kept her accessories minimal.

The model styled her glossy caramel tresses loosely in natural waves and bolstered her stunning features with a smokey-eye make-up palette and bold red lipstick.

She was seen looking as loved-up as ever with her husband Johannes, 45, who cut a smart figure in black trousers and a blue shirt.

The German model kept comfortable in a pair of white trainers and completed his stylish look with a grey bomber jacket.

The couple led the guest list at the celebration of PAIGE's spring collection, which saw the brand's founder Paige Adams-Geller in attendance.

Also among the guests were the likes of actress Nell Tiger Free, Isaac Carew, Isa Hernaez, cinematographer Alfie Hollingsworth and British Vogue's Sarah Harris.

Olivia and Johannes have been romantically involved for over a decade and have been married for eight years. 

They initially met through mutual friends and began dating in the late 2000s, before they got engaged in 2014 and tied the knot later that same year.

In January, Olivia took to Instagram to share an array of snaps as they enjoyed a trip to her partner's native Germany.

In the snaps, the smitten pair were seen enjoying their time at a resort on the island of Sylt.

The reality television personality began her post with a shot of herself and her husband posing in front of their lavish accommodation.

The happy couple flashed a pair of wide smiles as they took in the sights that the property had to offer.

She also wrote that she and her husband had 'such an incredible time living our best life' in the post's caption.
