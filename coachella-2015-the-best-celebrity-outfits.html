Coachella 2015: The best celebrity fashion

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 25, 2024

    Festival season officially kicked off with Coachella at the weekend, as stars from Kate Bosworth to Kendall Jenner headed to the musical extravaganza in California. Fashionistas from Gigi Hadid to Rosie Huntington-Whiteley were the epitome of cool as they rocked everything from printed maxi dresses to colourful denim skirts.

    Kate Bosworth was stunning in a printed jacket and dress

    Kate Bosworth is often hailed as the Queen of Coachella fashion and this year she did not disappoint. The 32-year-old actress turned heads in a striking cream jacket which featured beige panel detailing, crocheted three-quarter length sleeves, and a colourful embroidered pattern. She teamed the statement piece with a sleek cream dress which showed off her slender figure, completing the look with a cream Coach Rivet Dakotah bag and a pair of brown suede ankle boots with silver star-shaped studs. Meanwhile, jaws dropped when pop sensation Katy Perry arrived at Coachella pop-up the Soho Desert House. The singer rocked a black sheer top with pink floral detailing paired with a matching maxi skirt from Australian designer Alice McCall. She complemented the look with a pair of black sandals, and wore her hair in a halo braid for ultimate festival glamour.

    Katy Perry turned heads at Coachella pop-up the Soho Desert House

    Rosie Huntington-Whiteley also stopped by, looking elegant in a pale grey T-shirt paired with a pale mint denim skirt. Her accessories were on point - she rocked festival must-haves including a brown leather studded bag, a pair of tan ankle boots and a pearl necklace. As well as the show-stopping performances, Coachella is renowned for having some of the glitziest parties, and this year was no different. Moschino's Jeremy Scott hosted a celebrity-filled bash on Saturday night, with fashion's elite including Alexa Chung in attendance. The 31-year-old presenter wowed as she channelled boho chic in a black maxi dress with a floral print and long sleeves, paired with black patent shoes and a Chanel cross-body bag.
