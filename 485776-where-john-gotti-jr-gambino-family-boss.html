Where is John Gotti Jr now? Is he the Gambino family boss?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Throughout the 1980s and the early 1990s, John Gotti used his vast wealth and power to run the Gambino crime family in New York City. His leadership style made the Gambino crime family the most powerful organised crime syndicate in the United States until his arrest and sentencing in 1992. But since there was a void in the leadership, Gotti’s son, John Angelo Gotti, took over the leadership and became more private, unlike his father. But where is John Gotti Jr. now, and is he still the head of the Gambino crime family?

TABLE OF CONTENTS

John Gotti Jr. is an American former mobster. He is well known for being the acting boss of the Gambino crime family. Junior took over the syndicate's leadership from 1991 until 1999 when the authorities indicted him on racketeering charges.

He was only the acting boss during that period since his father was already serving life in prison after the jury found him guilty on several charges, including murder and racketeering.

Who is John Gotti Jr?

John is a former American mobster born John Angelo Gotti in Queens, New York, United States. He is the son of John Gotti (father) and Victoria DiGiorgio Gotti (mother). Furthermore, he has four siblings: Victoria Gotti, Angela Gotti, Frank Gotti, and Peter Gotti.

Unfortunately, Junior’s brother, Frank, died on 18 March 1980, aged 12, after a neighbour’s car ran over him. The neighbour who drove the vehicle vanished months later. Also, his father died in 2002 in prison aged 61 after battling throat cancer.

Who was John Gotti Jr’s father?

John Gotti Jr.’s father was an Italian-American mobster, gangster, and the head of the Gambino crime family. He ruled the streets of New York throughout the 80s into the early 90s.

He was later arrested, convicted, and jailed for life after the court found him guilty on 13 counts, including murder and racketeering.

How old is John Gotti Jr today?

As of 2024, the American former mobster is 59 years old. He was born on 14 February 1964. His zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Which school did John Gotti Jr attend?

The personality grew up in a two-story house in Howard Beach, New York. He attended New York Military Academy, a college preparatory, co-ed boarding school in Cornwall. After finishing his high school studies, he did not attend college or university. Instead, his father helped him become an entrepreneur and opened a business called Samson Trucking.

What does John Gotti Jr do for a living?

John managed his trucking company for a while and after the business failed, he went to work for the Carpenters Union. In 1985, John Gotti Jr.’s father became the boss of the Gambino crime family.

Since his father was now at the helm, Junior slowly became a rising star in New York’s underworld. Three years later, when he was in his mid-20s, Junior became an official member of the Gambino family in 1988 after his induction.

His father’s influence made him a crime family captain. As a result, Junior made history as the youngest capo or captain in the family’s history. The position forced him to learn the ropes of the underworld, including the rackets like loansharking. But what made him unique was that he was now a mob leader despite his young age.

As soon as he became a member and leader of the Gambino family, his father’s life on the streets ended. In 1992, the authorities charged and convicted his father of racketeering and murder.

Before his conviction, Gotti Jr. became the acting boss of the Gambino crime family in 1991. Like his father, he fell on the wrong side of the law.

In 1999, the authorities charged and convicted him of racketeering. He accepted a deal from prosecutors to only serve seven years in prison. Between 2004 and 2009, many cases, including old ones, came his way, and he became a defendant on many occasions. Unlike his father, all his cases ended in a mistrial.

In January 2010, his prosecutors dropped all his charges, and he became a free man. In 2015, he released his first book, Shadow of My Father. Several producers also released a film in 2018 called Gotti. In June 2018, a documentary TV miniseries titled Gotti: Godfather and Son aired. Junior participated in all productions.

Was John Gotti Jr an informant?

Gotti Jr. faced accusations of collaborating with the government as an informant. At the time, he was undergoing trial for racketeering and stock fraud charges that carried a potential sentence of 100 years.

Gotti expressed anger at the indictment, believing that he had immunity for the alleged charges due to a prior plea agreement with the U.S. attorney's office. In response, he declared his intention to feed the federal prosecutors useless or entirely false information, vowing never to testify against anyone.

His strategy aimed at securing a favorable plea deal while implicating two adversaries, reputed capo Daniel Marino and associate Joseph (Joe the German) Watts, with the federal authorities.

Despite its uncertainty to others, particularly his ex-underling John Alite, Gotti pursued this plan, resulting in him being criticized as a "rat."

Where is John Gotti Jr now?

As of 2024, he lives with his family in Oyster Bay Cove on Long Island’s North Shore in New York City. Even though he continues to be recognized as the son of John Gotti and a former member of the mob, John Gotti Jr. has been leading a private life as a family man since his trial in 2009.

Who runs the Gambino family now?

Until May 2019, Frank Cali served as the head of the Gambino Crime family. After Cali's demise, reports indicated that Lorenzo Mannino had assumed the role of the new Gambino leader, holding the positions of both leader and street boss but did not hold the official title of boss.

In 2021, it was revealed that Domenico Cefalù, an Italian-American mobster, had taken on the role of the boss for the Gambino crime family in New York City.
