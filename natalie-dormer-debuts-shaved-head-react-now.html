Natalie Dormer Debuts Shaved Head: React Now!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Natalie Dormer turned heads on the red carpet of the SAG Awards Saturday night.

And it was all because of the lack of hair on the head of this Game of Thrones star.

After three months of keeping her new look a secret, Dormer debuted a half-shaven dome at the event, one she’s sporting due to her role as Cressida in The Hunger Games: Mockingjay.

In the novel, Cressida is described as having “a shaved head tattooed with green vines,” causing us to wonder if the actress has more to take off in the near future.

 

“It was a bit weird lying in bed for the first few nights,” Dormer told EW of the buzzcut. “[One] side feels normal, [the other] side doesn’t feel so normal against the pillow. [And] hats fit differently.”

We’d imagine so.
