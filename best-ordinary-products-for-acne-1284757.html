Acne happens. Those dreaded pimples, clogged pores, and blemishes can wreak havoc on your skincare game. As you go through our list, you will find a lot of moisturizing and hydrating ingredients and products, instead of harsh exfoliants. 

As an acne sufferer, you may think moisturizing will make oily skin worse. Not true! Let us explain. Your skin makes excess oil when it's dehydrated — another reason why even dry-skin folks break out and get acne flares. Properly hydrating the skin can actually help regulate oil production and prevent breakouts, as per a study published on NCBI. A good moisturizer doesn’t add oil, rather it helps the skin retain water. Humectants like hyaluronic acid pull water into the outer skin layers, while emollients seal it in. Over time, the right moisturizing agents will help balance your skin.

    How long does it typically take to see results from using The Ordinary products for acne?

    It may take anywhere between a week to a few months to see a lasting effect — however, this is directly dependent on the products you are choosing to use and your skin condition and needs.

    Can The Ordinary products for acne be used during pregnancy or while breastfeeding?

    The Ordinary products have not all been tested for safety during pregnancy and breastfeeding and it can vary depending on the key ingredients in each product.     Not all The Ordinary products for acne are safe for individuals with sensitive skin. However, the products in their Acne Set are made of gentle ingredients, especially the cleanser. You may conduct a patch test before using it on a larger area.
