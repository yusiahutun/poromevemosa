Albufeira sex workers target Brits at Portuguese resort despite crackdown

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Albufeira sex workers continue to target Brit tourists despite a crackdown on seedy behaviiour at the popular Portuguese resort.

Rowdy holidaymakers have seen the town hit the headlines for the wrong reasons and locals have had enough of the lewd antics.

A recent headline in a Portuguese paper ran “Sex, drugs and violence”.

It talked of Brits walking around day and night “half naked and drunk” while “leaving a trail of destruction”.

It even reported a “shop mannequin being raped”.

A shopkeeper told Jornal das Noticias: “For many years I had a shop mannequin at the door but she was constantly being raped.

“First I tried to hide her inside. In the end I had to get rid of her as totally drunk British people often dressed in just thongs insisted on simulating sexual practices with her.”

Albufeira has long been seen as the tourist capital of the Algarve but its seedy image is now putting off families.

The town’s mayor Jose Carlos Rolo is trying to improve the image and he is working on a “code of conduct” where he would eventually like to see fines in place for bad behaviour.

So far there has been no improvement and sex workers continue to target randy Brits at the beach resort.

Online call girls offer their services in English with their rates an average of 150 to 200 euros.

“Kate”, an Italian, said: “Fun for me is about sharing a great time and experimenting with different sexual moods and ideas.

“I am available 24/7 be it for a special occasion or a fantasy experience.”

Another Portuguese worker called Ariana said “I am kind, flirty and very sexy.

“I’m into men, women and couples.”

With many call girls offering their business there is plenty of competition.

A Brazilian “Kelly” said “I am a very hot brunette with big butt full breasts, educated, affectionate and ready to satisfy your secret desires in bed.”
