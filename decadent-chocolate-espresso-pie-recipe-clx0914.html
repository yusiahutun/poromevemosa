Decadent Chocolate Espresso Pie

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

Inspired by Anna Jones's winning pie at the 2013 state fair of Texas.

Read More

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Yields: 8 - 10 serving(s)

Prep Time: 40 mins

Total Time: 7 hrs 40 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 1 1/2 c.

    milk, divided

  • 1/3 c.

    cornstarch

  • 3/4 c.

    sugar

  • 1/2 tsp.

    salt

  • 1 tsp.

    instant espresso powder

  • 3

    large egg yolks

  • 1 tsp.

    vanilla extract

  • 1

    (4-ounce) bar semisweet chocolate, chopped

  • 1

    (4-ounce) bar bittersweet chocolate, chopped

  • 4 oz.

    milk chocolate, chopped

  • 1/2

    recipe basic pie dough

Espresso Whipped Cream

  • 1 1/2 c.

    plus 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream, divided

  • 2 tsp.

    instant espresso powder

  • 6 tbsp.

    powdered sugar

  • Grated chocolate to garnish

Directions

  • Step 1Whisk together 1 cup milk and cornstarch in a small mixing bowl until smooth.
  • Step 2In a 3 1/2-quart saucepan combine sugar, salt, and remaining 1/2 cup milk. Cook over low-heat, stirring frequently until sugar is dissolved. Whisk in espresso powder and cornstarch mixture. Cook over medium heat until mixture thickens, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  • Step 3Whisk about 1/2 cup cornstarch mixture into egg yolks in a large mixing bowl. Whisk this back into espresso mixture in saucepan and cook over medium-low heat 5 minutes, or until mixture is thickened further.
  • Step 4Remove from heat and whisk in vanilla and chopped chocolate. Transfer to one prebaked and cooled Perfect Piecrust. Place plastic wrap directly onto filling (to prevent film from forming). Let stand 1 hour; then refrigerate for 6 hours.
  • Step 5Microwave 2 teaspoons cream in a small microwave-safe bowl for 10 seconds. Stir in espresso powder. Place remaining cream in a chilled mixing bowl. Beat at medium-high speed until soft peaks from. Add powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon at a time, until medium firm peaks form. Add espresso mixture, beating just until incorporated. Spread over chilled pie and top with grated chocolate.
