Jeremiahs Italian Ice to Open Next Month

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Jeremiah’s Italian Ice will have a new home in Stuart. The iconic shop plans to open its doors June 6, in the Downtown Stuart Shopping Center (860 SW Federal Highway), where locals will enjoy a menu packed with delectable frozen treats. 

The Stuart store will be family-owned and operated by business partners and first cousins Jason Kleiner and Danielle Bean. Kleiner, a former owner and operator of tech companies, and Bean, a stay-at-home mom and preschool teacher, have been longtime fans of Jeremiah’s. When the opportunity to franchise a location became available, Kleiner jumped at the opportunity.  

“We have been fans of Jeremiah’s for as long as we can remember and we’re excited to bring this concept to Stuart,” Bean said. “The brand’s family-friendly atmosphere and dedication to serving communities is definitely something we want to be a part of and we hope to become the go-to place for a family night out, after the big game, or if you’re just in the mood for a tasty treat.”  

Jeremiah’s frozen treats have swept across Florida and gained a loyal customer since opening in Winter Park in 1996. Each store boasts an upbeat atmosphere and offers more than 40 flavors of Italian Ice, as well as soft ice cream. Jeremiah’s Gelati combines the best of both, with layers of Italian Ice and creamy soft ice cream in nearly limitless flavor combinations.
