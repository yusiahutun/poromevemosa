Coco Austin receives backlash for wearing 'inappropriate' G-string bikini at a water park

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

Coco Austin has been on the receiving end of online criticism after she shared photos of herself in a G-string bikini from a family outing in the Bahamas.

The beauty has been slammed for wearing the 'inappropriate' swimwear after she posted numerous snaps from her day at Baha Bay Water Park in Baha Mar last week.

The model, 43, was at the resort with her daughter Chanel, six, and her husband Ice-T, 64, who took the sizzling snaps, with Coco joking that he turned into a 'paparazzi'.
