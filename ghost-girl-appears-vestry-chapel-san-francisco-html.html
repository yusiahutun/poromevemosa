Possible ghost of girl appears in the Vestry at the Chapel in San Francisco

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, April 5, 2024

Security video from a restaurant inside a San Francisco music venue appears to show the ghostly figure of a young girl in a dress running towards the door before turning and heading off camera. 

Footage from the Vestry restaurant at the Chapel music venue taken around 5am and then posted to YouTube shows the girl appear and stay in the shot for about two seconds before disappearing.

Employees at the Chapel, which occupies a Mission District building that was built in 1914 to serve as a mortuary, have reported seeing spirits and specters inside the historic structure in the past.

The janitor (who is seen in the video) and the rest of the cleaning crew who were working the night the girl appeared were 'terrified' by the footage and reported the little girl did not come with them.

According to Camille Ives, the Chapel's Marketing and PR Manager, staff members had 'no explanation' for the little girl who is seen in the video. 

Employees have previously reported hearing a little girl whisper, smelling perfume, faucets turning off and on without assistance, hearing footsteps in an empty room and other paranormal activity. 

The green room at the Chapel is also rumored to be haunted and some workers refuse to enter it.

An answer about the girl's identity may be on the way soon as a ghost-hunting TV show received approval to begin filming at the Chapel sometime in the near future.
