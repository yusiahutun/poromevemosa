Drake is a singer a songwriter a Grammy Award-winning artist a platinum-selling artist salesman and the face of a light-skinned nation. Drake was born Aubrey ‘Drake’ Graham on October 24 1986 to Sandi and Dennis Graham. He derives his African-American genes from his father a native of Memphis Tennessee and he owes his Canadian Jewish descent to his mother. On a lighter note, we know that Drake’s height cannot be attributed to his mother.

Drake’s interest in entertainment and shows has not started in recent days; it started very early. He was first recognized for his role in the popular TV series Degrassi: The Next Generation which he left in 2007 to pursue and focus on his rap career. After a few mix-tapes, he went on tour with rapper Lil Wayne in 2008 and was signed to his Young Money Entertainment label in June 2008 Drake was on roll and since then he remains just as relevant.

Drake has come a long way since then as he now holds the record for the most hits on Billboard’s R&B/Hip-Hop Chart. He also created a brand October’s Very Own (OVO) with subsidiaries such as OVOSOUND and OVOFEST. Drake is the proud owner of a clothing line and his own channel on ‘Beats 1’ he is currently an ambassador for ‘The Toronto Raptors ‘. Drake’s net worth is currently estimated at $55 million.
