Chania Genesis online booking, routes, parcel services, contacts

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 28, 2024

When it comes to long-distance travelling, passengers are always concerned about their safety and comfort during the journey. As a result, most bus companies have focussed on providing services that meet the customer's satisfaction. With Chania Genesis, you enjoy these plus much more if you want to travel along its routes.

Chania Genesis has existed for close to 10 years now. Over this period, it has established itself as one of the best in the passenger transport industry. The company also offers parcel services at affordable prices.

About Chania Genesis

Chania Genesis Company was founded in December 2011. It offers its customers passenger transport and parcel services. It operates on common routes from Nairobi to Malindi and Mombasa.

The company has modern fleets in the market. Its buses range from Scania, Mercedes Benz and Man buses to meet their customers' demands. They have daily morning and afternoon departures in all destinations with route pick up services.

To ensure that their customers are comfortable as they travel, the buses have the following features:

  • Sufficient legroom with charging systems
  • Two by two reclining sets
  • Free WiFi services
  • First aid services
  • Enough overhead luggage carrier
  • Air conditioner to some of their buses
  • TV services and music system

Chania Genesis routes

The bus plies the following routes:

  • Nairobi to Mombasa
  • Nairobi to Malindi
  • Nairobi to Thika
  • Nairobi to Nyeri

The company bring about stiff competition to other companies which serve customers on the same route. They have modern and unique fleets which are locally made in Kenya from the Top bus manufacturers.

Chania Genesis booking office

The bus company has offices in Nairobi and other major towns in Kenya. These offices serve most business functions of the company. For instance, apart from booking, you can also send parcels there.

Booking of the tickets is made at their offices which are available in all bust stations. You can also make your ticket booking by contacting their agents.

Chania Executive online booking

The bus company does not currently have online booking services. However, if you wish to book a seat, call their offices for directions.

How much fare does Chania Genesis charge?

The company charges different fares depending on the route passengers are travelling. The fares are also subjected to charges depending on various factors. If you want to know the amount you need to travel to any destination, you can call the office and inquire.

Chania Genesis parcel services

The bus company offers services across major towns in Kenya. These are the towns along its routes. Therefore, if you wish to send a parcel to Mombasa, Kilifi, Nairobi, and other cities where it has offices, this would be the best means.
