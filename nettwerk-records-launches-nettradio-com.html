Nettwerk Records Launches Nettradio.com

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 7, 2024

VANCOUVER, BC (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — On Tuesday, March 1st, Nettwerk Music Group launched Nettradio.com, a network of 5 online radio stations which each represent a genre of music comprising the independent label's extensive roster, which includes Sarah McLachlan, Delerium, Old Crow Medicine Show, Tiesto, and the Finn Brothers. The station will give listeners the option to click through to download individual tracks from the new Nettmusic.com MP3 download store, or to purchase CDs from Werkshop.com.

The March 1st launch coincides with the release of Ivy's In The Clear, which will be streamed in full throughout the day on Nettradio's New Releases station. Beginning March 2nd, the playlist will include music from Nettwerk's recent and upcoming releases, including The Devlins, Hermit, The Perishers and Coralie Clement. The remaining four Nettradio stations will broadcast Singer/Songwriter, Roots, Chillout, and Electronic music respectively, representing the full spectrum of Nettwerk music. Nettradio will also feature exclusive content including live performances, album previews, and interviews with Nettwerk artists.

To tune in to Nettradio, please visit www.nettradio.com
