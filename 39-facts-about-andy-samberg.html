Early Life

Andy Samberg was born on August 18, 1978, in Berkeley, California.

Comedy Beginnings

Andy Samberg started his comedy career performing sketch comedy with The Lonely Island, a group he formed with Jorma Taccone and Akiva Schaffer.

Saturday Night Live

In 2005, Andy Samberg joined the cast of Saturday Night Live (SNL), where he gained widespread recognition for his comedic skills and memorable characters.

Digital Shorts

One of Samberg’s major contributions to SNL was the creation of digital shorts, including the viral sensation “Lazy Sunday” and “D*ck in a Box.”

Awards and Nominations

Samberg has been nominated for numerous awards throughout his career, including a Golden Globe for his role in the television series “Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

Impersonations

Samberg is known for his impressive impersonations, including Mark Zuckerberg, Nicolas Cage, and Hugh Jackman.

Music Career

Aside from his comedy work, Samberg is also a talented musician and has released several parody songs with The Lonely Island, such as “I’m on a Boat” and “Jizz in My Pants.”

Film Roles

Andy Samberg has appeared in several films, including “Hot Rod,” “Popstar: Never Stop Never Stopping,” and “Palm Springs.”

Emmy Award Win

Samberg won an Emmy Award for Outstanding Original Music and Lyrics for his work on the SNL digital short “D*ck in a Box.”

Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Samberg played the role of Detective Jake Peralta in the critically acclaimed television series “Brooklyn Nine-Nine,” which ran for eight seasons.

Hosting Awards Shows

Samberg has hosted several awards shows, including the Primetime Emmy Awards and the Independent Spirit Awards.

Voice Acting

In addition to his on-screen work, Samberg has lent his voice to various animated projects, including the films “Hotel Transylvania” and “Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs.”

Collaborations with Justin Timberlake

Samberg has collaborated multiple times with musician Justin Timberlake, creating hilarious music videos and sketches together.

The Lonely Island Albums

The Lonely Island has released multiple comedy albums, including “Incredibad,” “Turtleneck & Chain,” and “The Wack Album.”

Internet Sensations

Many of Samberg’s digital shorts and music videos have become viral internet sensations, receiving millions of views online.

Comedic Timing

Samberg is known for his impeccable comedic timing, seamlessly blending slapstick humor with clever wordplay.

Political Satire

Samberg has often used his platform to engage in political satire, addressing current events through his comedy.

Celebrity Impressions

Andy Samberg is skilled at impersonating a wide range of celebrities, from Nicolas Cage to Justin Bieber.

Collaborations with Adam Sandler

Samberg has appeared in several films alongside Adam Sandler, including “That’s My Boy” and “Hotel Transylvania.”

Comedic Writing

Diverse Acting Range

Saturday Night Live Digital Short Explosion

Influential Comedy Figures

Hosting Saturday Night Live

Samberg has returned to host Saturday Night Live multiple times since leaving the cast, showcasing his continued dedication to the show.

Online Comedy Platforms

In addition to his success on SNL, Samberg’s comedy has thrived on online platforms such as YouTube and Vimeo, attracting a global audience.

Writers Guild of America Award

Samberg and The Lonely Island were honored with a Writers Guild of America Award for their work on SNL’s digital shorts.

Comedy Central Roast

Samberg was the Roast Master for the Comedy Central Roast of James Franco, delivering hilarious jokes and memorable zingers.
