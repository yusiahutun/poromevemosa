Kylie Jenner sends condolences to BFF Anastasia Karanikolaou after mother's passing

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Kylie Jenner was quick to send her heartfelt condolences to her best friend, Anastasia 'Stassie' Karanikolaou, after the model revealed that her mother has tragically passed away. 

The TV personality, 25 — who officially legally changed her one-year-old son's name from Wolf to Aire — jumped to the comment section of Stassie's post where she informed her follows of the sad loss. 

The mother of two, who has been close to the media star, 26, since her fallout with ex-BFF Jordyn Woods, penned, 'I love you forever.' 

Stassie uploaded an assortment of photos of her mother that had been taken over the years, and typed out, 'on monday june 26th heaven gained someone so special...,' but did not reveal the cause of her death. 

She continued, 'it's hard to imagine not being able to call you or hug you or cry at literally anything and then laugh at the fact that we're all such crybabies & so so much more..' 

Sending condolences: Kylie Jenner, 25, was quick to send her heartfelt condolences to her best friend, Anastasia 'Stassie' Karanikolaou, 26, after the model revealed that her mother has tragically passed away; seen in May in West Hollywood 

Sad loss: The media personality revealed that her mother had passed away with a new post on Instagram along with a slew of throwback photos that had been taken over the years 

Stassie emotionally concluded with, 'we’ll miss you everyday. we know you’re at peace now with papou.. we love you to the moon & back forever. rest in paradise mommy.' 

The first image that the star had shared in the Instagram carousel was a past image of her mother posing with both her and her older sister from when they were younger. 

The second photo had been taken as her mother held Stassie in her arms and gave her a loving kiss on her cheek. Another picture showed her mother glancing down at her with a loving smile when she was a little girl. 

The fourth image added into the photo series was a close-up snap of her mother as she flashed a cheerful smile towards the camera. Two other pictures were taken when Stassie was joined by her mother for photos when she was a young child. 

Lastly, the media personality uploaded a throwback photo of her mother when she was being walked down the aisle in an elegant, white lace wedding dress along with a sheer veil and sparkling tiara. 

Along with Kylie, other celebrities and close pals of the model took time to send their love and support to Stassie as she mourns the loss of her mother. 

Jenner's mother, Kris, typed out, 'Sending you so much love Stass and praying for you and your family ❤️,' while Khloe Kardashian wrote, 'Stass I am so so sorry. I love you so much. I am forever here for you for ANYTHING ❤️.' 

Hailey Bieber added, 'Love you Stass ❤️,' while Sofia Richie Grainge stated, 'I’m so sorry Stas❤️.'

Singer Justine Skye penned, 'Love you so much stas,' while Devon Lee Carlson typed, 'love you so much.' Amanda Steele added, 'I’m so sorry sending you so much love.' 

Other celebrities that sent their condolences on Friday included Billie Eilish's brother, Finneas, who wrote, 'So sorry for your loss stass.' 

During an interview with Bustle back in 2021, Stassie briefly opened up about her family and childhood, revealing that she grew up in a middle-class upbringing in Woodland Hills. 

'My mom struggles with addiction, so that was a big thing. We have a very distant relationship. I've seen her maybe a couple times in the past few years,' she explained to the outlet. The media star added that her father also used to work 16-hour days. 

'He did whatever he could to provide the best life that he could for me and my sister. He was there,' she stated. 

Stassie then opened up about the importance of friendship in her life, such as her bond with Kylie Jenner, whom she met at a Barnes & Noble. 'We know too much about each other. We're stuck with each other forever.'

The star also humorously joked that the two were, 'going to move to a farm together and get married,' and revealed that the secret to a long-lasting friendship was 'brutal honesty.'

While talking to E! News last year in August 2022, Stassie also gushed about her friendships and support system around her. 'All my friends help me.' 

'When we all start working on new collaborations or working on new products—or whatever it may be—we all send them to each other and get everyone's feedback, which I think is a beautiful thing...' 

The model added that, 'they want to make sure that what I'm putting out and what I'm doing is perfect. Some people don't want to see other people win. I've lucked out with my group of friends.' 

Earlier this month in June, Kylie celebrated Stassie's 26th birthday and the two appeared in a hilarious TikTok uploaded to the reality star's account.

The BFF's could be seen wearing stylish ensembles while standing closely together. At the beginning of the short reel, Stassie could be heard asking, 'What is this f***ing house tour that she [Kylie] is doing?' 

The Kardashians star then draped her arm around Stassie's shoulders and brought her into frame, with the model happily saying, 'Hi!' towards the camera. 

'My best friend is so funny. I like it when she shows personality, because she is hilarious,' the media star added. The two then shared a quick laugh and Kylie announced, 'It's Stass' birthday, she's 26 which means I'm nearly 26.' 

Towards the end of the nearly 30-second clip, Jenner added, 'Happy birthday, I love you so much,' which was promptly followed by more giggles and laughter.
