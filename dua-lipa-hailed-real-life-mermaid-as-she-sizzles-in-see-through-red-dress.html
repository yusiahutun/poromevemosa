Dua Lipa hailed 'real-life mermaid' as she sizzles in see-through red dress

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Dua Lipa has been providing all the content for fans since being in Albania, from bikini pics to stunning evening dresses, and she’s now ‘wowing’ fans as she sizzles by the sea in a gorgeous see-through red dress.

The music star rose to fame in 2015 and has had major success in her career ever since. Lately, she recorded the hit single “Dance The Night” for the Barbie movie, in which she plays the character of a mermaid. It looks like she’s now channeling her character in real life from her latest snaps.

Dua Lipa sizzles in red see-through dress

Taking to her 88.7 million Instagram followers, the One Kiss singer posted a range of photos as she posed by the sea in a stunning red floor-length dress.

Dua posed in various angles, showing off her toned body in the sheer netted dress.

The sunset can be seen in the background shining on the waves to create a holistic backdrop, as she captioned the post: “charging my crystals.”

The 27-year-old also wore a sheer dress to the Los Angeles Barbie premiere, as fans said she was “body goals”.

Fans say she ‘looks like a mermaid’

Dua’s fans rushed to the comments to compliment the star on her outfit, and her looks in general as some hailed her a ‘mermaid Barbie.’

“This Barbie is a mermaid,” penned one fan.

Another wrote: “You are a real mermaid Dua!”

“The prettiest ever,” wrote another.

“The most beautiful and hottest woman alive!” exclaimed one.

One screamed: “REAL-LIFE MERMAID!!”

Dua Lipa stars in Barbie

The chart-topping singer is multi-talented as as well as having a Top 5 track in the record-breaking Barbie film, she also plays Mermaid Barbie in Greta Gerwig’s movie.

Some fans may have to do a double take when they see the star perform as she can be seen sporting electric blue hair.

She joins the star-studded film alongside John Cena, who also plays the unlikely role of a mermaid.
