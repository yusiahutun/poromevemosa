10 most beautiful female boxers you should follow on Instagram

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Women are now beginning to take dominance in the professional boxing scene. What was previously seen as a men's thing has grown to accommodate more women. While it is possible to believe that women who prefer boxing are not the most gorgeous or feminine, that is not true, as this is home to some of the most beautiful female boxers.

Boxing history is full of many great female fighters, but the present times have made their presence more known. Even though most have topped different categories and divisions, their beauty remains unquestionable. In addition, their boxing prowess makes them outstanding.

10 most beautiful female boxers on Instagram

Women's boxing has turned out to be a major international sporting stage. The boxing world wouldn't be the same without the most beautiful boxers. But who are they? Here is a look at the top 10 most beautiful female boxers according to Total Shape:

1. Ebanie Bridges

  • Full name: Ebanie Bridges
  • Age: 36 years old as of 2023
  • Instagram: @ebanie_bridges

Ebanie is one of the most pretty female boxers from Australia. Before starting her kickboxing training, she practised karate until she was 13. She has bagged many state championship titles. For instance, in 2016 and 2017, she won a gold medal at the Australasian Golden Gloves.

In March 2022, she earned the International Boxing Federation female bantamweight title. Apart from her prowess and the coveted titles, she has always won the hearts of many with her underwear during weigh-ins.

2. Mariana Juarez

  • Full name: María Anastasia Juárez Trejo
  • Age: 43 years old as of 2023
  • Instagram: @barbyjuarez

Mariana is a Mexican professional boxer and one of the greatest female boxers who are models. She began her boxing career at 18, where she spent most of her time fighting various amateur bouts around Mexico.

  • WBC female bantamweight

3. Cecilia Braekhus

  • Full name: Cecilia Carmen Linda Brækhus
  • Age: 41 years as of 2023
  • Instagram: @ceciliabraekhus

Cecilia is one of the most beautiful female boxers you should follow on Instagram. She is a Colombian-born, Norwegian-raised professional boxer and retired kickboxer. She started kickboxing at 14, where she competed in many amateur matches.

Before turning professional, she recorded 75 wins, five draws, and zero losses. From 2014 to 2020, she was the undisputed champion in the welterweight category holding the following titles simultaneously:

  • World Boxing Association
  • World Boxing Council
  • World Boxing Organization
  • International Boxing Federation

4. Yokasta Valle

  • Full name: Yokasta Galeth Valle Álvarez
  • Age: 30 years old as of 2023
  • Instagram: @yokavalle_official

Yokasta is a Costa Rican professional boxer. She is one of the most cutest female boxers ever. Even though she was born in Nicaragua, her family relocated to Costa Rica when she was seven. As a result, she uses her matches to represent Nicaragua and Costa Rica.

Her father inspired her to become a boxer; she had her first match when she was 13. She is a world champion with the IBF female junior flyweight title in 2016 and the IBF female mini flyweight since 2019. BoxRec ranks her as the 4th best active mini-flyweight champion in the world.

5. Mikaela Mayer

  • Full name: Mikaela Joslin Mayer 
  • Age: 33 years old as of 2023
  • Instagram: @mikaelamayer

Mikaela is an American professional boxer. She bagged a bronze medal at the 2012 World Championships as an amateur boxer. In addition, she competed for the United States at the 2016 Olympics. She is a former unified super featherweight world champion.

Mikaela has been holding the WBO female title since 2020 and the IBF female title since 2021 before unfortunately losing both in October 2022. BoxRec ranks her as the 2nd best active female super featherweight in the world, while The Ring ranks her 3rd.

6. Paige VanZant

  • Full name: Paige Michelle Sletten
  • Age: 29 years old as of 2023
  • Instagram: @paigevanzant

Paige is an American mixed martial artist, professional wrestler, bare-knuckle boxer, and model. She is also one of the most beautiful female boxers you should follow. Previously, she was signed to the Ultimate Fighting Championship, where she participated in the flyweight division.

7. Erica Farias

  • Full name: Érica Anabella Farías
  • Age: 39 years old as of 2023
  • Instagram: @lapanterafarias

Erica is a talented Argentine professional boxer from Buenos Aires. She is a two-weight world champion for holding the WBC female lightweight title from 2011 to 2014 and the WBC female super lightweight title from 2014 and 2018.

While playing as an amateur, Erica bagged a silver medal at the World Championships in 2006. In addition, BoxRec ranks her as her 2nd best active female super lightweight player in the world while The Ring ranks her 4th for the same.

8. Lauryn Eagle

Lauryn bagged the WBO women's super-featherweight title two years after turning pro. Away from sports, she took part in the reality television show, The Celebrity Apprentice Australia. Also, she is a former Miss Teen International 2004.

9. Claressa Shields

  • Full name: Claressa Maria Shields
  • Age: 28 years old as of 2023
  • Instagram: @claressashields

Claressa is one of the youngest, most beautiful, and most successful American professional boxers and mixed martial artists. She has numerous world championships in three weight classes under her belt. Her championships include:

  • Undisputed female light middleweight
  • Undisputed female middleweight 
  • Unified WBC
  • IBF female super middleweight
