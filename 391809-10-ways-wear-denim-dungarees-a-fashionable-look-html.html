10 ways to wear denim dungarees for a fashionable look

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Denim dungarees are fashionable attires that make you look chic and trendy. They may have been out of fashion for a while, but as old styles make a comeback, the dungaree is also featuring in a big way. The denim pieces are not only excellent and iconic fashion pieces, but they are also extremely functional and comfortable, making them a win any time.

These fashion attires come in different styles and sizes. This means that anyone can find a favourite style to suit their preference. They include shorts, dresses, and long/pant dungarees. With such a huge variety to go for, most people will easily find a perfect match for themselves.

Top denim dungarees styles to go for

Are dungarees in fashion? Yes, they are back. Fashion enthusiasts have noticed that the old-school dungaree is massively making a comeback. It seems to be taking a new, funky, and more sophisticated look that matches today's wearer's needs. Most youngsters can comfortably rock a dungaree in their favourite colour without worrying about seeming out of place.

Besides colour differences, dungarees available today also come with different style preferences. Some of these are short, making them an excellent choice for those who want to show off a bit of skin in the leg. There is also the option of dresses and pant dungarees. In the end, one picks what works best for them. Below are a few different ways that you can rock your favourite denim dungarees.

1. Pair a dungaree dress with a striped shirt

Your favourite denim dungarees dress will need a trendy pairing. This is a perfect sun-dress option for when the weather is warm and accommodating. You can choose it in a mini or midi length, and pair it with a striped shirt for the perfect smart casual look.

It is excellent for when you want to hang out with friends. It can also make an ideal beachwear, as long as you bring your favourite beach sandals along.

2. Denim on denim for the win

The denim dungarees shorts are versatile and work with almost anything. However, pairing it with a denim long-sleeved or a denim coat helps you achieve the sexy all-denim look that is both classy and timeless.

Make sure to choose different colours for the perfect contrast. You can also opt to pair them with a jumper when the weather is chilly or cool. This can be matched with a pair of long boots in brown or black, depending on your mood.

Alternatively, wear shorts with a pair of printed T-shirts and great matching training shoes. This gives you a simple yet sophisticated look for the summer. You can also choose to wear a plain white T-shirt with your blue shorts dungaree, and have your hair in a messy bun or let it down. Throw in a pair of your favourite sunglasses to complete the fancy summer look.

3. Keep it casual chic

This is probably the most popular old-school dungaree look you will come across. Depending on what you are going for, you can choose to wear it with a printed short-sleeved shirt, a simple plain T-shirt, or a small short-sleeved crop top with a neck kerchief or scarf.

This is one look that you can play around with to attain the best for your look. It all depends on whether you want a modern look or old-school. Whatever you choose, make sure it flatters your body.

4. The undone look

If you are going for the sassy new look dungarees, this is one way you can do it. The undone look simply requires that you leave the top part of the dungaree undone. This can work for dresses, shorts, and long pants dungarees. It works best with the top being casual, which is why you should consider pairing it with a plain T-shirt.

5. Off-shoulder pairing

Whether you love pink denim dungarees or prefer subtle colours like blue and black, how you pair your dungaree is what matters. Consider the off-shoulder look, with one side undone for a sexy and fancy look. This would work for casual hangouts with friends over the weekend.

6. The winter-ready look

You can still rock your dungarees when it is cold if you are smart about it. Choose a colourful polo neck shirt to wear underneath the dungaree. This look is best achieved for pant dungarees in your favourite colour. You can then throw in a long sweater or a chunky coat for an extra warm feel. The important thing is to ensure that you are warm during this period.

7. Bralettes never disappoint

This look works for a super sexy evening out with your partner. You can wear your favourite denim dungaree with a laced bralette underneath it. Complete the look with strappy heels, and you are ready for a crazy night out with someone you love.

8. Show a bit of skin

This look is perfect for someone who wants to show off some skin but is afraid of having her tummy hang out. Choose a white crop top and pair it with your best denim dungaree. People get to see how smooth and flawless your skin is at the sides, but you also cover up the stomach area.

9. The good old tomboy look

You do not have to own a cropped dungaree to enjoy this fabulous glammed-up look. Wear your best pants dungaree and roll up the underside of each leg for this sexy look. Make sure you bring out those fabulous pair of heels that you have tucked away. You can also rock this look with a pair of comfortable sneakers.

10. Bring back the seventies with accessories

If you are going for an old-school look, then this is the perfect way to do it. Pair your flared dungarees with aviator shoes. You can also throw in a few seventies accessories such as your bag and jewellery for the perfect old school look. You can be sure that you will be turning heads because of how exceptional you will look.
