Kate Beckinsale is teary-eyed as mom rushed to hospital in health scare

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Actress Kate Beckinsale is emotional in her latest Instagram post, sharing snaps of her mother getting hospitalized and her cat’s picture who unfortunately passed away.

The the-50-year-old English actress lost her cat, Clive in June when she shared a heart-wrenching post writing: “Clive has died. I don’t have it in me to make a montage or even look at pictures of him yet. Every single part of my house feels like he should be in it. If anyone is expecting to hear from me, you may not for a bit and I’m sorry. My heart is absolutely and totally broken.”

Kate Beckinsale is teary-eyed as mom rushed to hospital in health scare

Kate Beckinsale’s mother Jude Loe had a health scare recently which made the Prisoner’s Daughter actress emotional as she opened up to her followers. The actress wrote, “Embrace joy. Accept grief. Wear feathers. Sing. Do what makes you feel happy even for a moment. Pair grief and sickness and fear with as much life and laughter and celebration as you possibly can.”

The actress shared a number of snaps of her mother in the hospital getting examined as well as her cat in the post urging fans to “love hard with those you trust. Spread love when you can.”

Kate has been the biggest cheerleader of actress Jude Loe

Actress Jude Loe has always been iconic and her daughter Kate seems to be the biggest cheerleader for her. The actress once shared an adorable interaction between them on Instagram where she shared: “There’s a lot of horrible s*** in the world. But my mum is nearly 73 and just got her first beanie BYE.” 

In the Instagram post, fans could see an adorable interaction between the mother-daughter duo where Jude is sporting a white beanie and Kate gushes over the beauty saying, “Oh my God you’re literally the cutest ever person, Ma you look so adorbs I’m dying.”

Kate walked in the path of Jude but carved her own legacy

Daughter of actress Jude Loe, Kate chose to walk in the same line of career as her mother but she carved her own legacy throughout her career.

Jude has acted in over a dozen of television shows including Inspector Morse, Yesterday’s Dreams, and Space Island One, exploring more into sci-fi genres at the end while Kate chose a diverse set of genres for her work including a period comedy film Love & Friendship, for which she received a nomination for the Critics’ Choice Movie Award for Best Actress in a Comedy.
