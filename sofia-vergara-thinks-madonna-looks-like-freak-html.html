Sofia Vergara thinks Madonna looks like 'a freak'

The claws are out as Sofia Vergara blasts plastic surgery (and brands Madonna 'a freak')

Actress Sofia Vergara has launched an extraordinary broadside against celebs who go under the cosmetic knife.

The hot-blooded 38-year-old Modern Family star slammed the trend of women having fillers and surgery to look younger.

And Madonna, who'll be 52 on Monday, was at the receiving end of her rant.

Freak: Sofia Vergara passes her scathing opinion of Madonna to the new issue of Esquire magazine

'L.A. is crazy. The women all look the same now,' she told Esquire magazine.

'That thing with the cheeks. Like Madonna. Who do they think they're fooling? It doesn't make them look young. You end up looking like a freak.'

Down to earth Sofia, who was born in Colombia and moved to Florida when she was 23, stated that she may one day 'tweak her eyes but has sworn off major surgeries.'

Having once thought about having a breast reduction she was talked out of it by her mother who said, 'God is going to punish you if you cut them,' frightening her enough to change her mind. 

And having been voted one of Maxim's top 100 sexiest women it appears she made the right decision.

But it's doubtful she's on Madonna's Christmas card list now.

Madonna has resolutely refused to discuss cosmetic procedures - which has done little to dispel the rumours.

Some claim she has had cheek implants, a new A-list phenomenon which gives a plumped-up look.

There have also been claims the mother-of-three has undergone a relatively new procedure called the ribbon lift.

The 30-minute treatment, which is available at Beverly Hills clinics for around Â£4,300, uses a dissolvable implant to lift neck tissues through a short incision around the earlobe.

Experts believe it would explain her lack of wrinkles, even if Madonna is unwilling to do so herself.

'I'm not against plastic surgery, I'm just against discussing it,' she said.
