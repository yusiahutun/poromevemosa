Han So-hee - Bio, Age, Height, Net Worth, Facts, Nationality

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, March 25, 2024

Table of Biography

Han So-hee is a television actress and model from South Korea. Han So-hee has been cast in various roles in TV series such as  Money Flower (2017), 100 Days My Prince (2018), and Abyss (2019).

Early Life

Han So-hee was born in Ulsan, South Korea, on November 18, 1994. Her birth name is Lee So-hee (Korean: 한소희) and she holds South Korean nationality. So-hee’s birth sign is Scorpio and she is currently 29 years old as of 2023.  She belongs to the Asian ethnicity and has her belief in Christianity religion. 

Unfortunately, the identities of her parents and their profession are unknown Similarly, she has not provided proper information regarding her siblings. However, she frequently posts pictures with her grandmother on her social media sites and she is very close to her grandmother. She built her interest in acting at a young age.

Han So-hee – Education

Regarding her academics, she attended the local school of Ulsan and completed her school education. She then joined Ulsan Girls’ High School and completed her high school education. Similarly, following graduation, she enrolled at Ulsan High School of Arts.

Professional Career

Initially, Han So-hee appeared in SHINee’s “Tell Me What To Do” music video in 2016. Later, she made her acting debut, portraying the role of Lee Seo-won in the Korean TV series named “Reunited Worlds” in 2017. That same year, she landed the main role of playing  Yoon Seo-won in the TV series “Money Flower.”

She was then seen playing the role of Kin So-hye in the tvN’s “100 Days My Prince” in 2018. Moreover, she starred in KBS2’s “After The Rain” and made an appearance in Roy Kim’s “The Hardest Part” in that same year.  She played a  supporting role in the 2019 tvN series “Abyss,” alongside lead actors Ahn Hyo-seop and Park Bo-young.

Caption: The poster of JTBC’s 2020 TV series, The World of the Married (Source: IMDb)

Moreover, she starred in JTBC’s 2020 TV series “The World of the Married” alongside Kim Hee-ae and Park Hae-joon in which she played the main role of Yeo Da-kyung, a young mistress. When the TV series ended, it was the highest-rated television series in Korean cable television history and she received widespread recognition thanks to the success of the television series.

Continued her successful acting career

She was cast along with Song Kang and was seen playing the role of Yoo Na-bi in the 2021  JTBC romance drama “Nevertheless.” During the same year, she appeared in the Netflix original crime-action drama “My Name” as a woman seeking revenge for her father’s murder.

She appeared as Lee Eun-soo in four episodes of the Disney+ mini-series “Soundtrack#1” alongside Park Hyung-sik. Moreover, she appears as Princess Kayena in a live-action teaser for the Kakao Webtoon series “The Villainess is a Marionette” in September 2022. The following year, she portrayed BTS’ Jungkook’s love interest in the music video for his track “Seven,” which was released on July 14, 2023.

Awards

Caption: Han So-hee wins Asia Artist Award (Source: Parapuan)

Being an actress, the South Korean actress has won a total of seven awards and eight nominations till now. She has won three Asia Artist Awards (2020, 2021, and 2o22), the Brand of the Year Award (2020), and the Korea First Brand Awards (2020). In addition, she was named the Actress of the Year (Domestic), in the 2021 Kinolights Awards.

Endorsements

As a model, she has worked with brands such as Ritz Crackers, CJ Group, Banila Co., L’Oréal, and Giordano International. She was picked up as the first Korean model for the British cosmetics brand Charlotte Tilbury Beauty in 2021. Moreover, she became a global brand ambassador for luxury fashion house brand Balenciaga and Swiss luxury watch brand Omega SA in the year 2022.

Moreover, she posed for Balenciaga’s 2023 Spring/Summer collaboration campaign with Adidas. She was announced as the global brand ambassador for Singaporean fashion house label Charles & Keith, French luxury jewellery house Boucheron, and Korean-owned sportswear brand Fila in 2023. Moreover, she was chosen as the new face of Chum-Churum Soju by Lotte Chilsung in 2023.

Net Worth 2023

Through her acting roles in TV series, and modelling career, she made a handsome sum of money. She also endorses different beauty products which also added to her net worth. As of 2023, her net worth is estimated at around $5 million. While she charges about $20k per episode to play in TV series.
