TikTok Woman Crashes Newly-Bought Mercedes Benz While Taking it on Drive

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 5, 2024
  • A TikTok video of a young woman proudly showcasing her new Mercedes Benz has gone viral
  • The footage shows the lady unveiling the car at the dealership and behind the wheel before sadly crashing her car
  • Many social media users responded to the post with heartwarming comments, hoping to comfort her for the misfortune

A young woman's excitement over her newly acquired Mercedes Benz took a disastrous turn when she wrecked it shortly after buying it.

A TikTok video shared by @gsibongiseni shows the woman excitedly unveiling her new Mercedes-Benz car at the dealership moments after finalising the purchase.

Woman damages Merc shortly after purchase.

The next part of the video shows the woman taking the driver's seat like a boss as she prepares to hit the road.
