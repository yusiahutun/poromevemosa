7 Snack Stadium Ideas Perfect for Your 2024 Super Bowl Party

Super Bowl Sunday is one of the best food holidays of the year. Super Bowl spreads rival Thanksgiving feasts, Christmas dinners, and Easter brunches. And if you prefer snacks to standard entrees, they truly can’t be topped. The question, of course, is how to display the Super Bowl snacks you’ve made. Standard snack spreads have their merits, but the most triumphant option is to create a Super Bowl snack stadium.

What’s a Super Bowl snack stadium? It’s a small replica of a football stadium—packed with Super Bowl snacks. Chips, sliders, and pigs in a blanket fill the stands like fans. Drinks surround the stadium, beckoning anyone who needs a beverage. And depending on your snack preferences, the football field can be made of guacamole, cake, or green grapes. As a host, you get bonus points if you craft edible football players. Super Bowl shots, cupcakes, and dip-friendly veggies all make great options.

While filling your snack stadium offers one challenge, building it presents another. You can piece together a DIY snack stadium using cut-out cardboard, paper snack trays, or downloadable snackadium instructions. Or you can save yourself the trouble, and score a premade snack stadium—like a wooden stadium serving tray, a paper stadium treat stand , or an inflatable football stadium , fit for a warm-weather Super Bowl pool party.

Our favorite premade snack stadium is this handy paper treat stand . It’s affordable, and the individual trays ensure your snacks are easy to organize. Plus, the snack stadium ships fast—perfect for last-minute Super Bowl party planning.

Whether you choose to buy or DIY, your 2024 Super Bowl snack stadium is sure to impress. And since a handful of hosts have already attempted the trend, you can use their best snack stadium ideas to make your spread a success and keep game day stress-free.

This DIY snack stadium is meticulously crafted from foam board and hot glue . Macy Blackwell (@macy.blackwell) even added team logos and sponsors to make the snack stadium look just like the real thing. The stands are filled with chips, waffle fries, hot dogs, Cheetos, and sandwiches—many of which pair perfectly with the guacamole football field at the snack stadium’s center.

This snackadium—pulled from the 2023 Super Bowl—features a plethora of game-day snacks. There are chicken wings, sliders, and individual cups of pasta salad. Another edge is lined with sweets, like football-themed cookies and chocolate-covered strawberries. And Jody Hoffman (@oilmygoodness) finished off the rest of the spread with pigs in a blanket, mini tacos, wraps, and soft pretzel bites.

This massive snack stadium from Samantha King (@niftymom) can handily feed a crowd. The DIY snackadium is loaded with veggies, chips, and pretzels you can dip in its guacamole center. And the outer stands are packed with candy—perfect for anyone with a sweet tooth. 

This simplesnack stadium is proof that your set-up doesn’t have to be complicated to be impressive. Mein Real (@mein_real) filled a premade snack stadium with candy, chips, pretzels, and popcorn—then stuck a few dips in the center for Super Bowl partygoers to enjoy.

Ready to put your DIY skills to work? Shannon Doherty (@athomewithshannon) crafted a snack stadium using plastic cups , cardboard boxes, wrapping paper , and a miniature football field . The eye-catching spread includes classic snacks, like chips, dips, and veggies.
