Who IS ChubbyEmu? Everything To Know On Doctor

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 16, 2024

ChubbyEmu is a doctor whose medical videos have made him a sensation on YouTube. Let’s learn more about him.

ChubbyEmu is a licensed medical practitioner who makes videos related to various medical issues for over five years.

ChubbyEmu Doctor on YouTube: Who is Bernard Hsu?

ChubbyEmu, doctor viral on YouTube, has the real name, Bernard Hsu.

Hsu has been making medical videos that aim to tell the medical stories of people worldwide. He provides medical insight into several incidents that occur to the people.

Bernard did not begin his YouTube channel with the internet of making medical videos. Instead, he had planned to publish funny and puzzle games content. His early videos were based on the game Nuclear Throne.

ChubbyEmu Age: How Old Is He?

ChubbyEmu’s age is 36 years old. 

Hsu was born on the 14th of September, 1984, in the United States. Notably, his birth sign is Virgo.

He created his YouTube channel at the age of 30 in September 2015. However, he began uploading medical videos only in 2017.

ChubbyEmu Wiki

ChubbyEmu’s wiki is not public.

His parents are from Taiwan. They came to the US in 1975, before Bernard’s birth. After coming to the US, his father pursued a degree in nuclear engineering, and his mother served for a bank.

ChubbyEmu has a sister with whom he was raised with many struggles and sacrifices from their parents. 

Bernard completed his high school education in 2002. Immediately, he joined three jobs at once so that he could support his family. Similarly, he graduated from the University of Illinois in 2006.

Moreover, he got his Ph.D. and became a Clinical Adjunct Professor.

Currently, his YouTube videos have got more than 137 million times having over 2.06 million subscribers on his channel, Chubbyemu.
