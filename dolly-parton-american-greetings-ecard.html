Dolly Parton Partnered with American Greetings for a Personalized Singing E-Card

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 25, 2024
  • Dolly Parton has partnered with American Greetings to allow fans to send a birthday e-card set to the tune of her iconic hit "9 to 5."
  • You can personalize the birthday greeting with any one of 1,200 different names.

Because she is simply the best ever, Dolly Parton wants to wish you and your loved ones a happy birthday.

The legendary country singer has partnered with American Greetings' "SmashUps" to allow fans to send a birthday e-card set to the tune of Dolly's iconic hit "9 to 5." You can personalize the birthday greeting with 1,200 different names or choose from terms of endearment like "honey" and "darlin'", or titles like "daughter" and "brother."

Click here to watch a sample.

The "9 to 5" birthday remix puts your recipient's name onstage in lights and includes a personal message from Dolly saying, "That's right, [insert name here], this one's for you!"

The Dolly fan in your life will love hearing their name on her lips, as well as all the clever, birthday-themed lyrical twists like:

"'Cause it's birthday time/what a day for celebrating/there'll be songs and gifts/and some happy congregating/yes, it's birthday time/with your friends and your relations/it's a time for joy and happy celebrations."

“Celebrating who you are and why you are special is something I have always encouraged and have known is important for my own identity,” Dolly says of her new project. “God made us all unique in our own ways, and I love the fact that I was able to offer something special for everyone. In a time like now, when we do not get the chance to share special moments like birthdays with each other in-person, I am so happy to know my ‘Birthday Time’ SmashUp ecard is now available to share with those you love.”

We can't wait to send this birthday e-card to everyone we know! After all, who doesn't love Dolly?
