Amy Schumer apologizes for saying Nicole Kidman looks like an alien in this photo

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Amy Schumer is facing the wrath of the fans as she apologizes after mocking Nicole Kidman’s new photo. The 42-year-old comedian later addressed her comments on social media.

The controversy between Nicole Kidman and Amy Schumer occurred after The Northman actress was seen attending the US Open and Amy took a jab at a photo of the Hollywood star. The Oscar-winning celebrity seems unfazed by Amy Schumer’s apparent snub, as an insider reportedly said that it is kind of silly and “she’s not fazed.”

Amy Schumer apologizes for saying Nicole Kidman looks like an alien

Amy Schumer’s Nicole Kidman post enraged fans as she posted a photo of the Eyes Wide Shut star with the caption, “This how humans sit.”

Later, Amy deleted the photo and took to Instagram to apologize for her previous comment. But she then took aim at another pair of celebs in her apology. In a now-deleted post, she wrote: “I want to apologize to all the people I hurt posting a photo of Nicole Kidman and alluding to her being an alien.”

Fans think Amy Schumer took a jab at Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis in her apology

While apologizing for her Nicole Kidman post, fans think that Amy Schumer also took a jab at Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher in her deleted post.

In the original apology, she also wrote: “I will be asking the cast of ‘That ’70s Show’ to write letters advocating for my forgiveness. #takingtimetoheal”. The Trainwreck actress was referring to the couple’s letter for ’70s Show star Danny Masterson, during his sentencing level.

Speaking of Amy’s apology, a user posted on X (formerly Twitter): “In an online apology after huge backlash for mocking Nicole Kidman, Amy Schumer then took aim at Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis. Much better!”
