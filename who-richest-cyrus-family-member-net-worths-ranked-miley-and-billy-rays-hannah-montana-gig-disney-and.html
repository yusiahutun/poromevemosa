Who is the richest Cyrus family member? Net worths, ranked from Miley and Billy Rays Hannah M

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 17, 2024
Although Billy Ray and Tish have gone their separate ways finding new partners (Billy Ray with Firerose and Tish recently becoming engaged to Prison Break’s Dominic Purcell), they both continue to make big bucks on their own terms – and so do their kids.

So, who is the richest Cyrus family member in 2023? Let’s take a closer look …

8. Christopher Cody – U$1 million

Billy Ray and ex-girlfriend Kristin Luckey’s son is way more low-key than his siblings. He works in law, according to his LinkedIn. The 31-year-old is a litigation associate in Austin, Texas, with a considerably smaller net worth of about US$1 million, as estimated by ECelebsBio.

7. Braison Cyrus – US$2 million

Miley’s brother is also a musician and he’s built a fan base of over 400,000 on Instagram alone. At 28, he’s been releasing singles since 2018 and has one album to his name, Javelina, which came out in 2021, putting him at an estimated net worth of US$2 million, per Newsunzip.

6. Brandi Glenn Cyrus – US$2 million

Brandi is the daughter of Tish and her ex-husband Baxter Neal Helson. Also in music, she works as a professional DJ and is a co-host of Your Favorite Thing and Sorry We’re Stoned podcasts, as mentioned on her Facebook page. The 35-year-old is also part of the band Frank and Derol, putting her fortune at a Celebrity Net Worth-estimated US$2 million mark.

5. Trace Cyrus – US$2 million

With US$2 million in the bank, per Celebrity Net Worth, Trace, 34, has followed in the footsteps of his dad Billy Ray in music making too. The Kentucky-born singer and guitarist still works with his Metro Music band, which is best known for the single “Shake It”. It also released a solo single “Pray at Night” in 2022. He has dated Disney stars like Brenda Song and Demi Lovato in the past.

4. Noah Cyrus – US$3 million

Miley’s little sister is just 23, but already has numerous hit tunes under her belt. As the second-most famous sibling of the Cyruses, Noah earned her US$3 million sum, per by Celebrity Net Worth, through songs like “July”, “Make Me (Cry)” feat. Labrinth and “Lonely”, to name a few. The Grammy-nominated songstress also voiced the main character Ponyo in the eponymous 2008 Disney animated film, starring alongside Hollywood bigwigs like Matt Damon, Cate Blanchett and Tina Fey.

3. Tish Cyrus – US$20 million

Everyone loves a good “momager”, right? As the proud mama and manager of daughters Miley and Noah, Tish knows a thing or two about the entertainment industry and clearly has her kids’ best interests in mind as she’s been in charge since the beginning of their careers.

While it’s unclear how big her cut is, her US$20 million net worth, per Celebrity Net Worth – combined with now ex-husband Billy Ray – is a good indicator of her savvy business dealings. But the website is yet to reveal her latest estimated fortune since her divorce from her long-time husband. It was revealed earlier this week that she’s newly engaged to Prison Break actor Dominic Purcell, a year on from her divorce from Billy Ray.

2. Billy Ray Cyrus – US$20 million

The Cyrus patriarch is arguably best recognised for being Miley’s dad on Hannah Montana, but in real life Billy Ray has also enjoyed a successful career in country music. The 61-year-old mullet-loving musician broke records with his 1992 chart-topping “Achy Breaky Heart” and lent his voice to Lil Nas X’s now-iconic “Old Town Road” remix in 2019.

Although he reportedly claimed to be a lifelong democrat, his song “We the People” was used in George W. Bush’s presidential campaign in 2000, states Celebrity Net Worth. His music, combined with his acting gigs from the Disney teen series to Jackie Chan’s The Spy Next Door, has landed him US$20 million in the bank.

1. Miley Cyrus – US$160 million

Miley Cyrus can’t and won’t stop making millions, apparently. The 30-year-old Tennessee native has come a long way since her days as a Disney darling and teen star in Hannah Montana. With hits like “Party in the USA”, “The Climb” and “Wrecking Ball”, she is now considered one of the pop music industry’s top talents, outshining even her father.

Miley has also raked in cash through other film gigs like Bolt, LOL and The Last Song, where she met her former husband Liam Hemsworth, from whom she divorced in 2020. She has also graced our screens in Netflix’s Black Mirror.
