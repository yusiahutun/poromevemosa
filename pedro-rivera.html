Pedro Rivera- Biography

Pedro Rivera is a well-known Mexican singer and actor.  He is also known as the Patriarch of Corrido and is regarded as a legend responsible for revolutionizing the music industry.

Pedro Rivera, often known as the Patriarch of Corrido, was born on the evening of February 23, 1943, in La Barca, Mexico. He emigrated to the United States in 1966, hoping to better his life and that of his family.

Pedro Rivera is a multi-talented actor, composer, producer, interpreter, and artist-producer. His talent and self-assurance helped him become a household name in a country where he had immigrated in the hopes of doing the same. Pedro has produced almost 1590 recorded songs and 50 productions, which is a remarkable accomplishment.

Pedro has even performed as a vocalist and judge in several musical performances in the United States and Mexico.
