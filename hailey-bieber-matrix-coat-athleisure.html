Hailey Bieber Pairs a 'Matrix'-esque Coat with Athleisure

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, January 1, 0001

Leave it to Hailey Bieber to put an unconventional spin on athleisure.

The model was photographed out and about in Los Angeles yesterday. She looked effortlessly cool in an all-black outfit, wearing a scoop bralette and flared yoga leggings from activewear brand Set Active. As a brand ambassador for Saint Laurent, Bieber layered up in a sweeping leather trench coat from the house's fall 2022 collection.

For some finishing touches, the Rhode founder opted for more black accessories, including a pair of puffy leather mules from Puma and narrow angular shades from The Attico.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

This season, Bieber has been upping the ante when it comes to colder-weather dressing.

Early last month, for instance, she made a string of appearances in New York City while wearing statement-making coats by Saint Laurent. During a coffee run with Justin Bieber, she bundled up in a long double-breasted cashmere coat in an earthy brown tone. She finished the ensemble with a black turtleneck, faded straight-leg jeans, black Marni loafers, and rectangular black sunglasses.

And during another outing with her husband, Bieber wore a speckled charcoal gray oversized coat, which included gathered sleeves and a belted waist. She styled the coat with a light gray sweater, black jeans, a light blue mini handbag from Bottega Veneta, black oval Saint Laurent shades, and her go-to Marni loafers.

Gotham//Getty Images

The model previously talked about her personal style for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story.

"I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said. "Even if I'm just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"
