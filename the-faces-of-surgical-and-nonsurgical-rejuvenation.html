The FACES of Surgical and Nonsurgical Rejuvenation

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 20, 2024

Craig M. Forleiter, MD, FACS and Shayna Fasanella, PA-C, Lickstein Plastic Surgery at Sanctuary Day Spa

Training and experience matter. For more than 20 years, the board-certified surgeons and award- winning aesthetic injectors at Lickstein Plastic Surgery have provided world-class aesthetic surgery and state-of-the-art nonsurgical rejuvenation treatment in a warm and welcoming atmosphere to more than 50,000 patients in Palm Beach County.

Sought after for its state-of-the-art treatments and natural-appearing results, Lickstein Plastic Surgery providers are equally well known for their likeable nature, compassionate care, and involvement in the community.

Now you can experience Lickstein Plastic Surgery in Port St. Lucie! Lickstein Plastic Surgery is proud to partner with Sanctuary Day Spa, a premier destination for relaxation and rejuvenation on the Treasure Coast, to bring its services to Tradition Square.

For over 18 years Sanctuary has provided the standard in pampering, offering a full range of aesthetician services: facials, massage therapy,
manicure and pedicure, as well as half- or full-day spa packages.

Lickstein Plastic Surgery at Sanctuary Spa offers a full range of cosmetic surgery options as well as neurotoxin and dermal filler injectables, Kybella, laser treatments including Sciton Halo, BBL & Micro Laser Peels, and Clear+Brilliant Touch, Ellacor skin tightening and wrinkle reduction and Coolsculpting Elite and Emsculpt Neo body sculpting. Ask about the proprietary Lickstein Lift and iLift nonsurgical rejuvenation procedures.

Appointments are available at the Tradition Square office inside Sanctuary Day Spa. For a customized treatment plan to meet your rejuvenation goals, call to schedule your consultation 561-571-4000.
