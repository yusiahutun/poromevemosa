Shay Mitchell Explains Why She's Started Wearing Diapers

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

This idea is either so insane it's brilliant, or it's just insane!

Shay Mitchell only just came clean about her pregnancy last month, but that clearly wasn't indicative that she was looking to be secretive about the whole process as she exposed way more than anyone expected about how it's affecting her daily life on set.

In the latest installment of her YouTube series "Almost Ready," the "Pretty Little Liars" star revealed that she'd come up with a rather surprising method to deal with the common pregnancy need to use the bathroom, like, all the time.

"Honestly guys, I've never known that somebody could pee as much as you do when you're pregnant," Mitchell said revealing that she'd resorted to wearing diapers while on set shooting.

"I swear to God, in a half an hour, I went to the bathroom probably about 22 times last night to the point where I was like, 'I'm f--king wearing diapers,'" Mitchell added.

The actress was in the middle of wardrobe fittings when she made the reveal, which is just one example of why she made a choice that some might find rather extreme. But for her lifestyle and the time demands of her job, this was the solution that made the most sense for her.
