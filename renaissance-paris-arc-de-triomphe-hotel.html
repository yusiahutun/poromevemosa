Renaissance Paris Arc de Triomphe Hotel

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Image courtesy of Renaissance Paris

Designed by Christian de Portzamparc, the five-star Renaissance Paris Arc de Triomphe Hotel is conveniently located just moments from the landmark for which it is named.

The chic Paris-meets-Indonesia interior design, executed by Era Studio, includes an interior private garden and brightly coloured lifts to whisk you up to your room. Dramatic drapery, shelves filled with unique pieces of art and sleek furniture also lend the hotel its modern and artistic vibe.

Image courtesy of Renaissance Paris

Accommodation

With balconies and large bay or floor-to-ceiling windows, some with views of the Eiffel Tower, the Arc de Trimphe or the Paris skyline, the upscale rooms are contemporary in look, spacious and tastefully decorated, and also equipped with the latest technology. Sleep soundly on thick mattresses with plush bedding featuring cotton linens and fluffy pillows.

Image courtesy of Renaissance Paris

Food and drink

Named after the capital of Indonesia's South Sulawesi province, the Makassar Restaurant uses spices that you will find today being traded in the city's harbour. They come together with only the freshest of French ingredients to create an innovative gastronomic offering, which you can enjoy against the backdrop of the hotel's garden. After dinner, sip a cocktail and nibble on bar snacks, such as homemade macarons or marinated shrimp.
