Contractor Victor Manta Bio, Age, Married, Height

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

Meet Victor Manta, a new contractor on HGTV’s No Demo Reno, who made a surprise appearance in the show. Learn more about his life beyond the TV show.

In this article, we’ve put together details regarding his age, marriage, height, and his family members. So, tag along with Victor’s Bio to unfold more details.

Meet Victor Manta, The New Contractor On No Demo Reno

HGTV’s No Demo Reno first aired in 2021 and the show premiered its second season however viewers have been charmed by the show’s host Jenn Todryk who makes it feels like we’ve been watching the show for a long time. Fans took notice when her contractor Tony Taveras wasn’t on the second season.

Instead, they met Victor Manta, a new kid on the block. In the opening shares, “Victor Manta is amazing. He’s one of my GC’s. He’s quick. He can adapt. He’s just a good fit”. He and his company specialize in commercial and residential remodeling, but we will learn more about it later.

This show is an opportunity to expand his business as he has to adapt to whatever Jenn throws in his direction. Hence, the show will be a testament to Victor’s skill as a general contractor.

As for the previous contractor, Tony shared on his IG in 2021 that he is looking to grow his company Linear Roofing with his business partner. They were interviewing new recruits to expand the business. So, it could be a good reason why Tony was absent in season 2.

And, he also has a professional gig as a builder on the side.

Likewise, in the first season David Piper, the master carpenter of the show also did phenomenal work. But, he was also absent from the show. In the first four episodes of season 2, fans haven’t seen David yet. Fans remembered him for his carpentry skills and charming personality.

And it appears that Victor, the new recruit, will be covering both of their jobs as a one-stop shop.

Ahead of the show’s premiere, on 24 June, Victor announced on his Facebook, “Tune in to @hgtv No Reno Demo tonight to see the ideas JJ had for this renovation!#norenodemo #hgtv”.

How Much Is Victor Manta Net Worth?

Victor Manta accumulated a total net worth of above $600K with income flowing from his construction company Manta Construction. Manta construction specializes in the construction of new homes from scratch. They can do it in any style, Mediterranean, Victorian, Spanish-style, and more according to clients’ preferences.

Manta construction also provides remodeling kitchens and bathrooms. They provide a full-service remodeling which provides electrical, plumbing, countertop, cabinet, and tile installation.

Besides construction and remodeling, they also specialize in disaster restorations. Their skilled contractors will restore a home to its pre-disaster status by working closely with the clients’ insurance company relieving them from the stress of working with the insurance company.

Finally, Manta construction also provides interior and exterior coloring services.

You can find Manta construction on their website, Instagram (@mantaconstruction), and Facebook (@mantaconstruction).

Victor Manta Age

Victor Manta, as of July 2022 is 31 years old. He was born in December 1990.

Is Victor Manta Married?

Yes, Victor Manta is a married man. He often shares on social media that his wife is his best friend. Her name is Jacqueline Manta (nee Araujo). According to The Knot, they were registered to be married on 26 July 2015.

The married couple is parents to two children; Jackson and Lara. A recent Instagram post shows Victor carrying their daughter while holding the hands of their son. “In all the work I’ve done over my career, nothing has compared to building a strong family,” he captioned.

Jacqueline was born to her mother Sandra Araujo (@sandra.araujo.581). Hailing from Texas City, she is currently based in Dallas, Texas. Jacqueline also has two siblings named Victor Arajujo and Bruno Araujo.

Find Jacqueline on Instagram (@themantaclan) and Facebook (@jackiemanta).

Victor’s parents are likely separated. Valdi’s relationship status on Facebook reads “It’s complicated” meanwhile, Solange reads married. Solange is a Brazilian who hailed from Recife, Brazil. She attended Escola Professor Moacyr de Albuquerque. She attended Dallas College Brookhaven Campus.
