Wife of ex-Cardinals VP Bill Bidwill Jr. 'cracked him on the head with glass cup during dispute'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Former Arizona Cardinals executive, and the brother of its current owner Michael, Bill Bidwill Jr. was allegedly struck in a violent dispute with his wife earlier this month. 

According to police reports obtained by TMZ, Bidwill told Paradise Valley Police Department he was hit in the head with a glass cup June 9. 

Per the report, Bidwill's wife, Nicole, is alleged to have broken the glass on him during an argument wherein she 'hit him with her hands about a hundred times' after he wasn't home for the arrival of carpet installers. 


Bidwill allegedly said Mrs. Bidwill was upset he hadn't been home for the arrival of the installers and when he did return, he poured himself a glass of beer but she snapped at him before he could drink it and ultimately cracked the glass over his head, causing it to shatter. 

Mrs. Bidwill was arrested and reportedly booked on one count of assault June 10, though her husband declined to press charges. 

Nicole Kugler Bidwill was arrested following an alleged dispute with her husband on June 9

Michael Bidwill - the brother of Bill Jr. - took over the team following Bill Sr's (R) passing in 2019

The Arizona Cardinals released a statement to TMZ, asking for people to respect their privacy

The Arizona Cardinals shared a statement with the outlet. 'Two weeks ago, Nicole Kugler Bidwill was involved in an incident that resulted in her arrest,' the Cardinals said. 

'This is a difficult time for their family but her husband and children love her very much and are thankful that she is currently receiving the help she needs. 

'They ask that their family's privacy be respected as they deal with this challenging personal situation.' 

Bidwill Jr. served as the Cards Vice President for multiple years while his father, Bill Bidwill Sr. was at the helm. 

Bidwill Sr. passed away in 2019 and his other son, Michael, is now the principal owner of the NFC West franchise. 

The Bidwill family first bought the Cardinals in 1933 while they resided in Chicago. The team moved to St. Louis in 1960 before moving to its current location, Arizona, in 1988.
