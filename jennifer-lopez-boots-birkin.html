Of Course Jennifer Lopez Would Match Her Sky-High Boots With Her Birkin

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Jennifer Lopez’s street-style looks are almost guaranteed to feature a cameo from her wide-ranging collection of Hermès Birkin bags.

Case in point: The Hustlers star was spotted out and about in Los Angeles yesterday wearing yet another monochromatic outfit, accented by a matching Birkin, of course.

The multi-hyphenate looked ready for fall in a suede jacket and miniskirt set awash in a rusty brown. The jacket featured a brown leather collar as well as shiny gold buttons down the center and on the cuffs. Underneath, Lopez layered with a white long-sleeved top.

For her footwear, she chose a pair of knee-high platform leather boots in a buttery caramel. The sky-high boots perfectly matched her choice of Birkin, which showed off the same warm brown over a glossy croc-embossed surface. She finished off the outfit with gold-rimmed shades and small gold hoop earrings.

Lopez pulled off another monochromatic moment the day before. She ventured out in a head-to-toe taupe look consisting of a clingy turtleneck top from Intimissimi, the lingerie brand for which Lopez is a global brand ambassador, plus soft cargo pants, boots with a thick block heel, and yet another Hermès Birkin bag—though this one came in a pale brown. She again accessorized with gold hoop earrings and some tortoiseshell cat-eye sunglasses.

The actor talked to Bazaar earlier this year about her ambassadorship with Intimissimi. “I feel connected to Intimissimi,” she said. “They encourage women to feel confident in their skin and, more importantly, to embrace that feeling!”
