20 gorgeous mother of the bride hairstyles for a chubby face

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 26, 2024

You have seen your daughter grow up, and she is now about to take that big leap into marriage life. The blissful occasion is one of a kind, and the bride's mother must appear extremely beautiful at the event. A perfectly put-together look includes a gorgeous hairstyle, which is not too over the top. So, what are the most beautiful mother of the bride hairstyles for a chubby face?

At a wedding, the bride and groom are the centres of attention, and their outward appearance matters a lot. The ideal dress veil, colourful bridal bouquet, matching bridesmaids' outfits, and an outstanding mother of the bride hairstyle are some of the most crucial details of the momentous event.

Therefore, the mother of the bride should fit the part perfectly. So, what hairstyle should the mother of the bride wear?

Amazing mother of the bride hairstyles for a chubby face

Mums should feel elegant, beautiful, and be the best version of themselves on their daughter's wedding day. And the best way of doing this is rocking a gorgeous hairstyle. Remember that you will be looking at the photos of your daughter's wedding day for years, and it is integral that you look glamorous. So, what are the best mother of the bride hairstyles that suit a chubby face?

1. Twisted updo

The twisted updo is perfect if you are looking for a retro mother of bride hairstyle to rock on your daughter's special day. The hairstyle goes well with oval, round and square shapes and is good with fine to thick hair. The style also fits well with a black-tie wedding.

2. Twisted half up, half down

The twisted half-up, half-down hairstyle is for mothers who like to wear their hair down but still want to update their look for their daughter's big day. This timeless classic is a winner, especially for a mother of the bride with a chubby face.

The highlight of the unique hairstyle is the simple knot holding your hair at the back of your head. You can accentuate the look with any accessory you desire to fit your face perfectly.

3. Side parted curly look

Keeping it simple is the most important thing with a mother of the bride hairstyle. And no other style can actualize this outcome better than the side-parted curly look. This chick look is simple yet elegant.

The hair is parted at the side and swept over so that the curly hair appears on one side of the face. It is perfect for a chubby face, and you can even style it how you desire to suit your attire. The look helps the face stand out.

4. Long thick red waves

The long thick red waves are one of the best wedding hairstyles for long hair and a chubby face. It is a simple mother of the bride hairstyle that doesn't require much work. All one needs to do is part the hair to the side and let it be. The style is perfect if you have long hair and want to have it up for the big day.

5. Elegant knot

The elegant knot is the perfect choice if you are searching for the best mother of the bride hairstyles for round faces. It is an effortless style that will bring the mother of the bride elegance without shouting too much.

You can achieve the look by sweeping your hair back into a twisting knot at the back of the head. The best way to accomplish the look is to keep the knot low, covering the nape. You can leave some light bangs on the side of your face to make it look lively.

6. Short flip hairstyle

The flip hairstyle never gets old and looks as elegant as ever. The hair is flipped upwards rather than curling downwards. Another great thing about the hairstyle is that you can have it in any colour you desire. The bangs are slightly shorter than the rest of the hair, making it a great mother of the bride hairstyle for a wedding.

7. Intricate twists and knots

The intricate twists and knots hairstyle is one the bride's mother can go for while attending their daughter's wedding. As the name states, it is an intricate style. However, don't let it fool you, as it is simple to create and will look elegant as always.

Take three sections of your hair from each side of the head to achieve this look. Twist and pull back to create knots. Let some bangs fall around the face to ascertain you have hair upfront.

8. The elegant bun

The elegant bun is your go-to style if you are looking for elegance on your daughter's big day. All you need to make this work are clips, thin elastic bands, doughnut bun bands, hair pins and a string of pearls for the decoration.

To achieve the look, you must split your hair into three sections, with the centre being the biggest. Then clip the sides away and tie the centre section into a high ponytail. Finally, lift the ponytail and use other accessories to create an elegant bun.

The best thing about this look is that it is something you will do in a few minutes. You can also do it yourself rather than relying on an expensive stylist.

9. French braid bun

The French braid bun needs no introduction as it is one of the classiest styles that have stood the rest of time. The bride's mother will look amazing with this simple yet elegant style that only requires a comb, elastic band, and hair pins to hack.

All one needs to do is to flip their hair over and in front and then start braiding from the nape to the neck. You can then tie it into a high ponytail and twirl it around into a neat bun. Finally, hold the hair into lace with hairpins and accessorize as you desire.

10. Pinned-back blowout

You may have limited styling options if you have short hair. But don't worry; you have numerous tweaks that can work for you, especially during your daughter's big day. The pinned-back blowout is one of the best solutions.

It is a great option for people who do not want an updo. And the best thing is that the hairstyle will inject an element of glam into any outfit, thereby effortlessly making you look amazing.

11. Side-swept curls

The side-swept curl is a chic look. You can easily rock it by styling your hair into classic curls and pinning them to the side for a formal or casual appearance. It is upon you to choose the side you want your curls to fall.

12. French braid updo

The French braid updo makes the perfect mother of braid hairstyle. It is perfect for long hair as it can withstand whatever the day throws at you and still look amazing.

13. The chignon knot

The classic chignon knot complements any mother of the bride outfit and is easy to style. Put your hair into a knot at the nape and wrap it around to create a chic bun. You can add plaits to make it look creative.

14. Blonde bob

The blonde bob is one of the classiest mother of the bride hairstyles for round faces. To accentuate your appeal, you can choose a centre-parted or side-parted bob filled with blonde hues. For the best look, ascertain that you have minimal jewellery so your hair can stand out.

15. Bouncy curls

The bouncy curls are perfect for the mother of the bride. The wavy hairstyle with golden reflection looks amazing and is great for ladies with chubby faces. The chunky curls to the layers of your hair add volume and bounce.

16. Braid-wrapped bun

The braided wrapped bun is one of the most elegant mother of bride hairstyles for a chubby face. It flaunts someone’s look in a cool and modest way.

17. Classic ponytail

Indeed, the ponytail is a classic look. As the bride's mother, you have no excuse not to look amazing. All you must do is smoothen up your hair with a nice styling product and tie it up into a ponytail by ascertaining that all your hair looks perfectly in place.

18. Voluminous swept back half up

If you want to feel fancy and look amazing, you can go for the voluminous swept-back half-up hairstyle. All you need to do is to provide a wavy texture to your hair and swipe it towards the back in totality. Then, tie a few layers into a half-up fashion to finish the style.

19. Wavy bob

Nothing is better than rocking a wavy bob to your daughter's wedding. It is the ultimate mother to the bride hairstyle that looks great on virtually everyone. All you need to do to have this style is to pump up the volume in your bob by creating wavy textures on the layers of your hair. Then, accessorize the hairstyle with long dazzling earrings, and you will love the outcome.

20. Messy bun

A mess can look great if done correctly and on the right head. A messy bun is one of the simplest mother of the bride hairstyles that can fit anyone. The style is perfect if you have long hair and don’t want to have it excessively styled on our daughter’s big day.
