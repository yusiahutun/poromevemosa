His hit single song titled “Brainwashed” was positioned at number 89 on the Billboard Hot 100 list in August 2021. In his music, he often discusses controversial political and social topics in the United States.

His musical influence artists are the Beatles, Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd, Joe Cocker, Black Sabbath, Marilyn Manson, Janis Joplin, Eminem, Aerosmith, Tupac, Kanye West, The Offspring, and GG Allin.
