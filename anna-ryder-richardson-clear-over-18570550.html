Anna Ryder Richardson in the clear over tree fall

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 22, 2024

The star, 48, had denied failing to keep visitors safe at her animal park.

But her husband Colin MacDougall, 46, admitted several breaches of health and safety laws.

Gruff Davies-Hughes, three, and his mum Emma, 28, were badly hurt when a branch fell on them at Manor House Wildlife Park in Tenby, Pembrokeshire.

MacDougall will be sentenced at Swansea Crown Court next week.
