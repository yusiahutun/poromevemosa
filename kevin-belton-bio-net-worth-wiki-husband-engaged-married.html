Kevin Belton’s Wiki

Kevin Belton was born in New Orleans, the United States. His father belongs to a French, American, and English ancestry. However, his mother’s origin is French-Caribbean. He holds an American nationality and belongs to mixed ethnicity.

The information regarding his birth year, birth date, and siblings are not known. However, some sources claim that he was born in 1960, which makes him around 59 years as of now.

It would not be weird to say that the talented chef Kevin was so much interested in cooking since his tender age. All that is, he started learning different types of food due to her mother. Her mother was the one who inspired him to pursue his career as a chef. He began cooking when he was just 7 or 8 years old.

After completing his education in Hotel Management, Belton started teaching the foundation of Louisiana named New Orleans School of Cooking, where he conducted over 7000 lessons of cooking. He taught at the foundation for more than 20 years and still going on. When people ask him how he makes the dish so fast, he concluded his mission saying:

“Everybody says you make it look so easy, but I teach them that it is easy. My mission is to get you to cook and then to sit down with your family or invite a friend over and share that meal. People need to get back to the table.”

Currently, Belton hosts New Orleans Cooking With Kevin Belton under the production of WYES-TV. Well, he has also made his appearances on several cooking programs including Emeril Live, Ready Set Cook, Live Love Luch and Taste of America.
