The top 10 most expensive sunglasses in the world in 2021

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Quality and price is a significant consideration before purchasing any product. However, this is the reverse for the rich. The brand type and price inform their decision to buy anything of a product they want. Some of the world's most expensive sunglasses are from Porsche brands like Chopard, Dolce and Gabbana.

In the past, the primary role of sunglasses was to protect eyes from harmful UV ray. However, this has continued to change over time as they have evolved into something more. They have combined functionality with fashion.

Most expensive sunglasses

For some people, having a high status means everything. Similarly, their sunglasses ought to be equal to the task as well. As a result, such people end up purchasing some of the most expensive sunglasses.

1. Chopard sunglasses – $400,000

The maker first showcased these glasses in 2012 at a Dubai fare. The main highlight is the perfect fitting of diamonds to form a clear surface of pure diamonds yet maintaining its original look. Some of the celebrities who have been spotted with a pair of these expensive spectacles include Tom Jones, Gwyneth Paltrow, and Elton John.

2. Dolce and Gabbana DG2027B sunglasses – $383,000

The Dolce and Gabbana DG2027B glasses cost just as much as your dream car or mansion costs. The sunglasses are a blend of classic modernity and a Mediterranean touch. These are the most sought after glasses by celebrities like Madonna, Nicole Isabella Rossellini, Kidman, and Kylie Minogue.

3. Shiels Emerald sunglasses – $200,000

The Shiels Emerald sunglasses is one of the most sought-after pair of jewellery in the world. They are made from Emerald lenses, which took over five years to outsource and three months to polish. The sunglasses design and finishing were inspired by Nero, the Roman emperor who had more than enough green gemstones at his disposal as a sign of wealth.

4. Cartier Panthere glasses – $159,000

Cartier makes some of the world’s most expensive designer sunglasses. The French-based luxury goods maker deals in exclusive jewellery, including sunglasses and watches. The brand attracts both celebrities and top people in business who flaunt their wealth in quality and expensive goodies.

5. Luxuriate Canary Diamond glasses – $65,000

The Luxuriate Canary Diamond glasses are made in Los Angeles by Franco Inc. The first pair was created in 2004, comprising of a diamond frame and gold. A buyer can choose between two styles, namely the Luxuriator Uno and the Luxuriator Due.

6. Bulgari Flora sunglasses – $59,000

What better way to flaunt in style than to splash $59,000 for a pair of sunglasses? The Bulgari brand has a presence in over 300 locations worldwide. These glasses give you high-end status and class.

7. Gold and Wood 253 Diamond glasses – $55,000

The Gold and Wood 253 Diamond glasses are one of the most expensive shades you can find in the market today. They are made by a Paris-based designer from wood and gold, as the name suggests, giving them both prestigious and luxury status.

8. Gold & Wood 119 Diamond glasses – $30,000

These pair fall in the line of most expensive sunglasses brands due to their manufacture and design nature. It comes with a unique combination of rare items and noble materials, including valuable timber to make the frames. Every piece is a masterpiece that tells the story of elegance and style.

9. Lugano Diamonds sunglasses – $27,000

Lugano Diamonds are some of the most expensive pair of sunglasses in the world. The pair comes with a gold frame and are fitted with diamonds on the frames. One type has 2.85 carats of pink diamonds, while another has about 3.59 carats of yellow and black sports diamonds.

10. Cartier Paris 18k Gold sunglasses – $25,000

Are you looking for sunglasses with expensive frames? Carter Paris 18 Gold sunglasses are the right fit for you. With these glasses, you will look stylish and trendy.
