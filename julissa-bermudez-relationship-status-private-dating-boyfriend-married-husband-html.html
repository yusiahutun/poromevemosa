Being a celebrity is tough at times as each of their life happenings becomes a public affair.

No matter how they try to keep the media and paparazzi away, they eventually get caught. Here's an actress who tried her best to keep the affair private but couldn’t prolong the privacy after 2014.

Romance With Maxwell In Details

An American Television personality, Julissa Bermudez and boyfriend, Maxwell reportedly started dating long ago. In 2011, the photos of the duo circulated in the web after Maxwell helped Julissa celebrate her birthday.

However, the "Make It Happen" star kept denying the rumors regarding the affair. Julissa shared in an interview with Mashonda the same year saying she and Maxwell were only good friends. She said,

He’s a friend. It’s the perfect example of how media loves to pin people together to make a story. I take a picture with someone and of course I’m automatically “dating” them.

She also tried justifying her words further by telling that she didn't share any romantic ties with anyone.

I am SO single. Absolutely. It feels good. I’m in a good place in my life.

Well, despite her endeavors, the relationship became public in 2014 after they were spotted canoodling in the beach of Miami. Everyone got assured after looking at those pictures that the couple was cooking something inside.

Julissa Bermudez and Maxwell spotted together at Miami Beach in 2014 (Source: bossip.com)

And finally, in 2016, the couple became open about their relationship. They attended the 2016 BET Awards together and posed for the media as well. They walked on the white carpet and sat in the audience throughout the show.

Julissa and Maxwell were posing at the BET Awards in 2016 (Source: thebf.com)

However, their relationship may not be what everyone expected it to be. Back in 2018, Julissa attended an interview with Power 92 Chicago where she talked about her relationship with Maxwell.

During the meeting, she claimed that the two had been friends for over a decade and that she valued his friendship. Further, Julissa also mentioned about the ongoing romance rumors between the two.
