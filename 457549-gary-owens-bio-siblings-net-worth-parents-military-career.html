Gary Owen's bio

Gary was born on July 26th, 1974, in Cincinnati, Ohio, United States. He is well-known for his show The Gary Owen Show. He is the son of Gary Owen and Barb Randall and was raised alongside his six siblings.

Who are Gary Owen's siblings?

The American comedian has six siblings; Emma Owens, Kyle Soto Randall, Kayla Owens, Michelle Truss, Ashley Muska, and Dallas Edward Randall. Gary Owen's family spent a significant part of their lives in a trailer in Oxford due to poverty.

The family had hard times making their ends meet. Then, to add to the misfortune, they lost one of their sons, Dallas Randall, due to a heroin overdose.

What does Gary Owens do for a living?

Gary is an actor and stand-up comedian. However, before this success in entertainment, he served in the United States Navy. He worked for six years as a Master-at-Arms and was in the Presidential Honour Guard.

Owen's passion for comedy was so strong that he decided to create a career. When he began a career in comedy, he was named the Funniest Serviceman in America. He captured the hearts of many, and his fan base began growing.

Gary's career breakthrough happened after winning the Funniest Black Comedian in San Diego contest. He attracted the interest of The Comedy Store in Hollywood and was offered his first gig. He got a place as a stand-up comedian in the BET comedy show called Comic View.
