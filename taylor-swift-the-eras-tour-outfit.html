Taylor Swift Shuts Down the Stage in a Shimmery Bodysuit and Knee-High Boots

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, October 3, 2024

Taylor Swift has returned to the stage, and to no one's surprise, her show is bigger and better than ever.

The songstress had an epic opening show for her The Eras Tour in Glendale, Arizona. It has been years since fans got to see Taylor in concert, and she did not disappoint. As if her songs weren't enough, the 33-year-old pulled out all the stops with entertainment, from diving from the stage to ever-changing backdrops. Taylor also showed off some seriously gorgeous outfits throughout her three-hour show.

We saw gowns:

John Shearer//Getty Images

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

And incredible two-piece sets:

John Shearer//Getty Images

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

But our favorite was the bedazzled bodysuit that was covered in colorful gems. Taylor wore this while she sang a few of her more emotional songs, like Lover and Cruel Summer.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

To complete the look, she wore matching silver knee-high boots.

John Shearer//Getty Images

Taylor's look was a custom piece by Atelier Versace, while the boots were from Christian Louboutin. She also wore designs by Roberto Cavalli and Oscar de la Renta.

Needless to say, when fans got a look at Taylor's show, comments like this flooded TikTok and Instagram:

  • "Queen."
  • "Literal Barbie."
  • "She is glowing."

Taylor will continue her tour through August, and we just can't wait to see what else she has in store for fans!
