Hamish Harding family: Who is his wife Linda Harding and stepson Brian Szasz?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 23, 2024

HAMISH HARDING was named as one of five passengers on the missing OceanGate submarine in June 2023.

The UK businessman was aboard the vessel when it vanished while exploring the wreck of the Titanic ship, 3,800 meters below the surface of the North Atlantic Ocean.

Who is Hamish Harding's wife, Linda?

Businessman Hamish Harding was married to his wife, Linda, before it was announced he and the other passengers on the missing Titan are believed to be dead.

On Thursday, June 22, 2023, OceanGate announced the likely death of its CEO, Stockton Rush, along with the remaining passengers.

"We now believe that our CEO Stockton Rush, Shahzada Dawood and his son Suleman Dawood, Hamish Harding, and Paul-Henri Nargeolet, have sadly been lost," the statement reads.

This announcement comes following the discovery of a debris field near the Titanic wreckage.

Linda and Hamish Harding were based in the UAE together, where Hamish ran a commercial and private aircraft company known as Action Aviation.

The couple shared two sons, Rory and Giles.

Linda also has a daughter named Lauren and a son called Brian.

Who is Hamish Harding's stepson, Brian Szasz?

Hamish Harding's stepson, Brian Szasz, was the first person to confirm that the businessman was one of the passengers on the submarine.

What happened to Hamish Harding?

Hamish Harding was one of five people to be reported missing on June 19, 2023, after a submarine visiting the wreckage of the RMS Titanic in the North Atlantic Ocean vanished.

The Titanic sank on its maiden voyage to New York on April 14, 1912, after the ship hit an iceberg and split in two.

It is known as one of history's major nautical disasters and was the subject of the 1998 film starring Kate Winslet and Leonardo DiCaprio, 

French submersible pilot Paul-Henry Nargeolet, chief executive and founder of OceanGate Expeditions, Stockton Rush, Pakistani millionaire Shahzada Dawood, and his son Sulaiman, are all believed to be dead.

Hamish posted to social media about going on the expedition the day before the crew took off.

He reportedly spoke to his friend about poor weather conditions just hours before the vessel vanished.

Nasa astronaut Terry Virts spoke about Hamish on Good Morning Britain, revealing that Hamish had texted him hours before he went missing.
