Meet Rishia Haas, The Ex-wife of Chef Andrew Zimmern. Interesting Facts about Rishia

Rishia Haas is best known as the ex-wife of successful culinary expert Andrew Zimmern. Her ex-husband hosts several popular TV shows such as Bizarre Foods with Andrew Zimmern, Bizarre Foods America, and others.

In addition, Richie works as a beekeeper at the MN Bee Lab. Let’s learn more about her education, career, net worth, and more.

Rishia Haas Early Life And Education

Rishia Haas was born in Champlin, Minnesota, United States of America. Her nationality is American and his race belongs Caucasian. Rishia attended high school in Anoka, Anoka.

After graduation, Haasa continued her studies at Augsburg University in Minneapolis.

Career: A Former Employee At The Retreat

She worked as an admissions coordinator at Alive with the Recovery. The rehab center in Wayzata, Minnesota, provides supportive drug recovery programs for those struggling with dipsomania and drug addiction.

After leaving The Retreat, Haasa began working at the Hazelden Betty Ford Foundation as an administrative assistant. Founded in 2014, this addiction organization is an addiction research center and graduate school that focuses on how to overcome addiction.

Rishia And her Husband Andrew Dated For Over 2-Year Before Marriage

According to sources, Rishia and Andrew met in the late 1990s. They both worked at a Minnesota cookery school. The couple married on 26 December 2002.

A month later, they held a post-Christmas party where they got married. Their relationship, unfortunately, ended after 15 years in 2018. The reason for the separation is unknown.

How Did Their Relationship End?

Cimmern had a dark past. He had once become homeless due to alcoholism and drug addiction. He had been on a dangerous path since his early teens.

“My parents had been divorced for six or seven years at that point, and it was sort of the seminal event of my life.”

And, as he said, Andrew and his wife Richie have a good relationship. He celebrated Mother’s Day 2020 by posting a beautiful photo of his late mother and wife on Instagram.
