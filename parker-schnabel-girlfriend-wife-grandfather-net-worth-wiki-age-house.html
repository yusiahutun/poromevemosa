Parker Schnabel Bio, Age

Parker Schnabel was born on July 22 1994 in Haines Alaska where he spent most of his childhood with his brother Payson.

His parents are Nancy and Roger Schnabel. Parker Schnabel’s father and grandfather are well-known miners. Roger is also in the construction and ownership of Southeast Road Builders.

Parker’s Career

The star of the reality series was interested in mining from an early age which is not surprising since his father and grandfather are also in the industry.

He reportedly used his tuition fees to create his own mining company. Subsequently, his exceptional expertise in mining earned him a spot in Discovery Channel’s Gold Rush television series. In the fourth season of the series, Parker found 1029 ounces of gold and in the fifth season, he found a large amount of 3362 oz of gold worth nearly $3.7 million. In total it is estimated that he extracted more than $13 million in gold.

After participating in the show since its debut in 2010 Parker announced in 2017 that he will play in his own three-part docuseries Gold Rush: Parker’s Trail.

According to People, the show will feature young Parker and his team as they tackle Alaska’s great wilderness in the footsteps of former gold miners.

The series which is already in its second season saw Parker and Rick Ness attempt the Klondike trail through Chilkoot Pass and up the Yukon River to Dawson City the very heart of the 1897 Klondike Gold Rush.

Parker Schnabel and his adventurous crew will be looking for a concession of virgin gold and in doing so will face challenges that could take the form of meeting dangerous animals that will not blink their eyelids before attacking them for dinner.

Parker’s new series is produced by Raw Television for Discovery Television Dimitri Doganis while Ceri Smith is the production director and Peter Campion alongside Mark Carter a series producer.

Matthew Vafiadis and Bill Howard are also the executive producers of Discovery Channel and Jessica Mollo is also an associate producer.

There’s no record of Parker’s marriage or divorce. However, he has a girlfriend named Ashley Youle. The couple has been together for some time now.
