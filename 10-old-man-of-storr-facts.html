FAQs

What is the Old Man of Storr?The Old Man of Storr is a prominent rock formation located on the Trotternish peninsula of the Isle of Skye in Scotland. It consists of a large pinnacle of rock that rises steeply from the ground, creating a striking and iconic silhouette against the sky.

How was the Old Man of Storr formed?The Old Man of Storr was formed through a combination of ancient volcanic activity and subsequent erosion. The towering rock formations are the remnants of volcanic plugs, which were exposed and sculpted by glacial and geological processes over millions of years.
