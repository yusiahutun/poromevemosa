What’s the difference between a mattress topper and a mattress pad? 

While mattress covers work to protect your bed from allergens, microbes, dust mites, and pet dander, toppers provide extra cushioning — even more so than mattress pads, which are usually thinner and designed to attach to your bed like a fitted sheet.

A mattress topper, on the other hand, is typically several inches thick and sits on top of your mattress. The best mattress toppers for back pain and hip pain are typically made of a variety of materials, including cotton, memory foam, and latex, and range in size from twin to California king, with queen size being the most popular. You can also get them for cribs and children’s beds.

What are the most comfortable mattress toppers for back pain and hip pain?

The best mattress toppers for back pain and hip pain are crafted with care to relieve joint and muscle soreness. They can even help ward off allergies — all the mattress toppers on our list are hypoallergenic.

Whether they are made from memory foam, latex, or down, these mattress toppers offer solid value and help create a soft, inviting bed that fosters relief from pain and more rejuvenating rest. Pay close attention to thickness, softness, and support when deciding on a mattress topper that will be comfortable for you.

Here are a few other things to be on the lookout for when shopping for the best mattress toppers for back pain and hip pain:

  • Certifications: Whether you’re concerned about harmful chemicals or the humane treatment of animals (down mattress toppers are made from duck and goose feathers), certifications such as the Responsible Down Standard (RDS) and CertiPUR-US ensure you feel good about your new mattress topper. The Parachute Down Mattress Topper is RDS-certified, and the UTTU Respira and Molecule Airtech mattress toppers are both CertiPUR-US-approved, so you can rest assured they contain no toxic materials.
  • Care instructions: We love a mattress topper that’s machine washable and safe to throw in the dryer, but many aren’t. If ease of laundering is a consideration for you, be sure to check the product specifications. The Molecule Airtec mattress topper has a washer-and-dryer safe mattress cover, making it easy to care for. (Check out this helpful article on how to clean a mattress, as well.)
  • Temperature regulation: Night sweats disturbing your sleep? Look for a cooling mattress topper, like the LUCID Ventilated Gel Memory Foam mattress topper.
