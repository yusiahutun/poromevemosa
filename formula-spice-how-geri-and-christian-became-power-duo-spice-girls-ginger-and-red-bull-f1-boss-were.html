Formula Spice: how Geri and Christian became a power duo Spice Girls Ginger and the Red Bull

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Below we take a look at how the relationship has different entertainment worlds colliding.

Geri Horner’s Spice Girl roots

The Spice Girls rose to fame in the 1990s with their debut album, Spice, which became the bestselling album ever by a female group. Their first two albums produced several No 1 hits.

While the Spice Girls have reunited at times, Geri has a number of other interests now.

The group reunited for tours in 2007 and 2018. It is estimated that the Spice Girls made US$75 million per year at their peak in the late 1990s, and Geri reportedly had a net worth of US$40 million in 2018. She has also appeared on TV shows, such as Sex and the City, and as a guest judge on American Idol and The X Factor.

She also has a business and writing empire

Geri has a series of yoga and exercise videos, a signature swimsuit collection, and has written several books. In addition to two autobiographies, she has also written a series of popular children’s books.

Geri was recently cast in an upcoming racing movie.

Geri was recently announced as part of the cast for the upcoming film Gran Turismo, based on the racing video game. David Harbour, Archie Madekwe and Djimon Hounsou will also star in the movie.

Enter F1 king, Christian Horner

In the late 1990s, Christian was a – ahem – wannabe F1 driver who realised he wouldn’t make it to the top circuit. So at 25, he retired from racing and moved into team management. Six years later, when Red Bull bought the Jaguar F1 team, they named Christian the youngest team principal – the fella in charge – in F1 history.

He is also one of the biggest stars of the Netflix docuseries Drive to Survive

When the Netflix docuseries Formula One: Drive to Survive launched in 2019, it introduced the crazy and glamorous world of F1 to a new audience and grew the sport’s popularity, especially in the United States. While other team principals were sceptical of the behind-the-scenes intrusion, Christian embraced it and immediately became one of the biggest stars, thanks to his bold and cheeky personality.

For some, Drive to Survive was an introduction to this sports-music power couple.

When Geri first appeared on Drive to Survive, many new F1 fans’ jaws dropped as they were first realising that she is married to Christian. It was almost too perfect that F1’s spiciest character was married to a Spice Girl.

Christian was previously married to Beverley Allen

They were married for 14 years and have one daughter together. Geri had several high-profile relationships before Christian.

Geri has a daughter Bluebell, 16, from a previous relationship with filmmaker Sacha Gervasi. She has also been linked to Robbie Williams, Russell Brand, Fred Durst, Duncan James and Lee Ryan, and was also briefly engaged to shipping tycoon Fabrizio Politi.

A spicy start to their relationship …

The couple’s relationship got off to a, dare we say, spicy start. They were first spotted dating in 2014 under eyebrow-raising circumstances. This was just a few months after Christian separated from Allen and only six months after the birth of their daughter.

But Geri and Christian had been seen together before. They were photographed arm in arm at an F1 race in 2011 with Christian looking somewhat uncomfortable.

They were seen near each other as early as 2009. Before the 2009 Monaco Grand Prix, Geri was photographed with former F1 boss Bernie Ecclestone. Christian can be seen hovering in the background. In 2014, one of Geri’s friends told British media that the Spice Girl had been “following” Christian for a few years.

Some also noted that the pair looked close a year before they became an official item.

According to British media, the couple were holding hands at a 2013 British Grand Prix at Silverstone, while Allen was six months pregnant at the time.

A Red Bull Racing crew member told the British media in 2014 that the 2013 British Grand Prix was “really weird” as Geri “just sort of appeared … everywhere”.

Geri and Christian Horner got married in 2015

The pair announced their engagement nine months after first being seen dating. They tied the knot six months after that, in May of 2015 in St. Mary’s Church at Woburn in southern England.

Guests at the wedding included people from the entertainment world, such as Baby Spice, Emma Bunton. British singer Myleene Klass was also photographed at the wedding. There was also racing royalty, including Jackie Stewart. Former F1 drivers David Coulthard and Niki Lauda were also in attendance.

They had their first child together in 2017

In 2017, Geri gave birth to a son, Montague, her first child with Christian.

The blended family has also been featured on Drive to Survive.

Geri has seemingly settled in nicely to family life and motherhood. In 2022, she shared a photo with her now-teen daughter at the European Women’s Championships, supporting the England Women’s National Team.

The Horner family share two homes … and plenty of animals

The family has homes in Oxfordshire and Hertfordshire, England. The Oxfordshire abode is featured prominently in Drive to Survive. In addition to being a doting mother, Geri also loves her animals.

The Oxfordshire estate has sheep, horses and other animals on the grounds. But the couple still has time for each other.

The pair took a helicopter to see a Paul McCartney concert on a date night. There are also date afternoons at Wimbledon. They also like to get gussied up for film award shows.The recently attended the 2023 BAFTA Film Awards at The Royal Festival Hall.
