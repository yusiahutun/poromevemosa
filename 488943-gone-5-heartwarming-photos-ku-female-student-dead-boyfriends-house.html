Gone too Soon! 5 Heartwarming Photos of KU Female Student Who Was Found Dead in Boyfriend's House

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 5, 2024
  • Victoria Muthoni Theuri was found dead in her boyfriend's house in Kahawa Wendani after going missing
  • The KU university student left home on Christmas Day after telling her parents she had gone to visit her sister
  • TUKO.coke brings you heartwarming photos of the young woman whose life was cut short

Victoria Muthoni Theuri was a young beautiful woman who had her whole life ahead of her which has sadly been cut off by the cruel hand of death.

She was found dead in her boyfriend's house just days after she went missing and her story has since broken hearts on social media.

TUKO.co.ke takes you through the deceased's heartwarming pictures:

1. Victoria Muthoni's Gorgeous smile

Going through her photos, it is easy to conclude that Victoria Muthoni was a smiling machine and she let her smiles light every place that she went.

2. Her beauty

The young lady was a beautiful woman whose loveliness shone through and made her stand out.

Not only were her looks beautiful, but she also had a kind look on her face that showed she was beautiful on the inside and the outside.

3. Victoria's disappearance on Christmas

The Public Policy and Administration student was last seen alive on Christmas Day after she left her parents' home in Kahawa Sukari.

She told her folks she had gone to visit her sister but on the evening of the same day, her parents realised she did not go to her sister's place.

4. Found dead in boyfriend's house

On Thursday, December 29, police officers found the young lady's body in her boyfriend's house in Kahawa Wendani's Alvo House.

5. Investigations into her death

Investigations into Victoria Muthoni's death are ongoing.

Victoria Muthoni's boyfriend denies wrongdoing

As reported, the man was traced and arrested for grilling at Ruiru sub-county Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI) headquarters.

He said he left her by herself on Christmas when he travelled to his rural home but investigations to determine her exact cause of death are still ongoing.
