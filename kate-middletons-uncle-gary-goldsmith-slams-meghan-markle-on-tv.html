Kate Middleton's uncle Gary Goldsmith slams Meghan Markle on TV

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Kate Middleton’s uncle, Gary Goldsmith, has slammed Meghan Markle on TV as he competes in a reality show in the UK.

The younger brother of the Princess of Wales’ mother, Carole Middleton, joined a host of stars for Celebrity Big Brother. Within 24 hours of arriving in the big house has slammed Meghan Markle in front of thousands of fans. Kate Middleton nor the royal family have spoken out about her uncle joining the TV show.

Kate Middleton’s uncle Gary Goldsmith isn’t a fan of Meghan Markle

The 58-year-old spoke to iconic host Sharon Osbourne on the show and he wasn’t afraid of speaking his mind. He also dived into what it’s like being an uncle to a royal who will be the next Queen on the British throne.

Gary Goldsmith claimed Meghan Markle changed the dynamic within The Firm, he suggested her husband will be back ‘one day’.

He seems to think the Duke of Sussex “will come back and be part of the gang again one day,” despite not being involved with The Firm himself. The 39-year-old lives in his Montecito mansion with wife Meghan and their two children, thousands of miles away from Buckingham Palace.

He told Sharon: “I have an opinion that Harry was really, really really loved and when they were a threesome, so Kate, William, and Harry they looked really comfortable together.

“Then suddenly there’s an extra dynamic that comes in, puts a stick in the spokes and creates so much drama that I don’t genuinely think is there, and rewrote the history, saying how unhappy he was and I just don’t think that’s fair”.

Gary Goldsmith has quite the opinion on Meghan Markle even though he confessed he’s never met her.

He added: “I mean you can’t throw your family under the bus in such a dramatic style, then write books about it and expect to be invited round for Christmas.”

“I think it’s really sad. At some point, I think he is going to come back and be a part of the gang again. He might need to,” he added. “We are a very forgiving nation, it would be nice if he did.”

‘They don’t understand it’

While 71-year-old Sharon had a more diplomatic approach to the conversation, not outwardly saying too much about the Sussexes.

She said: “I think [Harry] is foolish but he is not a bad person.

“In the beginning, they were very very well received.”

“They don’t understand it, but they do love it, you know? But it’s dwindling, dwindling, dwindling and there’s been some deals that they have done that haven’t worked well.”

It comes after it was rumored Kate Middleton’s uncle was planning to write a bombshell memoir to put claims from Harry and Meghan to bed.

He first teased plans of writing it all down back in 2018. But he hasn’t responded to the new rumors.

We’ll have to see if he’ll dish more details about The Firm over the next week of the TV show.

We doubt Prince Harry and Meghan Markle are watching though, sorry Gary Goldsmith.
