M&amp;S launch new elderflower gin liqueur filled with 23 carat gold flakes for 15

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 19, 2024

Looking for a tasty new drink to impress your family with?

Marks and Spencer’s are here to help.

The British retailer has released a fancy new beverage to add some luxury to your summer cocktails.

The “Glitter Globe” Elderflower Gin Liqueur combines some well-loved flavours with sparkling gold leaf.

Delicious and stylish…

Plus, it’s not too expensive at jut £15 for a 70cl bottle.

Marks and Spencer posted a pretty shot of the drink to its Facebook page.

M&S said: “Meet the drink of the summer: our new elderflower gin liqueur Glitter Globe!

“Filled with edible gold leaf, it tastes just as good as it looks.

“Try topping up with Prosecco to make our ‘We’re all gin this together’ cocktail – the ultimate stay-at-home sip.

“Find it in store when you’re shopping for essentials.”

Gin fans are thrilled and took to the comments.

One person wrote: “It’s great topped up with Prosecco.

“A lovely lady who works in your Grantham store recommended it this way.”

While a second added: “I purchased this today, the bottle is fabulous.”

A third commented: “Mmmm sounds delicious and looks good... look forward to some of that in the sunshine.”

Some worried that they would be unable to find the gin liqueur in their shops.

This was because of a similar release at Christmas where shops repeatedly sold out of the product.

M&S assured customers that they had ordered in lots of stock.

The supermarket said on their website: “Try simply served over ice with a squeeze of lime, topped up with tonic, or alongside our new summer Conte Priuli prosecco (which comes in an equally eye-catching silver bottle).”

Now, that does sound like a treat!

However, it’s not the only fun gin stocked by M&S at the moment…

They’re selling a magical colour-changing gin by Edinburgh distillery The Old Curiosity.

Just add tonic to see it change before your eyes.
