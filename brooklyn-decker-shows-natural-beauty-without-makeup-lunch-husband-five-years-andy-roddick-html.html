It was five years to the day that they were married.

However, on Thursday, Brooklyn Decker stepped out looking very casual for a lunch date with her beloved husband Andy Roddick in Los Angeles.

The 27-year-old model-turned-actress showed her flawless complexion while grabbing sushi with 31-year-old former professional tennis player Andy.

Natural beauty: Brooklyn Decker ventured out without makeup on Thursday for a wedding anniversary sushi lunch with husband Andy Roddick

Brooklyn also showcased her lean pins in a grey, short-sleeved T-shirt dress.

The former Sports Illustrated swimsuit cover model wore her long blonde hair straight and down.

She completed her simple look with a pair of pointy black Oxford shoes.

Andy kept it casual also in faded blue jeans, a grey long-sleeved zippered shirt, sunglasses and black cap.

Holding hands: Brooklyn wore a simple and short T-shirt dress and held hands with Andy after lunch

Love match: Andy and Brooklyn have been married since April 2009

Going long: Brooklyn showcased her lean pins in the short dress and had her long blonde hair down

The former world number one player and last American to win a Grand Slam singles championship back in 2003 held hands with his wife as they celebrated five years of marriage.

Brooklyn recently landed a regular role in the CBS sitcom Friends With Better Lives that also fetures James Van Der Beek and Kevin Connolly.

The blonde bombshell has been making the rounds promoting the comedy and recently appeared on a talk show with Larry King and also The Late Show With David Letterman.

Model looks: Brooklyn showed her flawless complexion without makeup

Walk and talk: Brooklyn chatted with Andy after their lunch date

Tall and taller: Andy and his six-feet two-inch frame stood tall over five-feet nine-inch Brooklyn

Handsome couple: Andy and Brooklyn were both blessed with good looks

Brooklyn also taped an appearance on Thursday night on Chelsea Lately and talked to host Chelsea Handler about shooting love scenes.

'Watching your make out face is the weirdest thing ever because you feel like it's sexy and it's not sexy,' Brooklyn told Chelsea.

Brooklyn and Andy were married on April 17, 2009 in Austin, Texas.

The actress has said they met after 'chivalrous' Andy had his agent call her agent to arrange a meeting.
