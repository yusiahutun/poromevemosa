LMCo. Quilted Trivet Laurel Mercantile

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, April 13, 2024
  • Made in the Mississippi
  • 7" x 7"
  • Assorted quilted fabrics 

Our LMCo. Quilted Trivets are handmade, uniquely patterned, and useful for preventing scratches and heat damage on fine surfaces. 

Because each trivet is unique and will vary in pattern and color, we do not offer pattern selection on orders. Each order will be a surprise pattern. The trivets are made from vintage quilts and as such may slightly vary in size or show signs of wear. These features make each piece unique, and we think that makes them even more special!
