Best Dogwood Punch Recipe - How to Make Dogwood Punch

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 12, 2024
Brian Woodcock

Throw a classy Easter or Mother's Day party by serving up a batch of this tasty punch, full of fresh fruit and citrus.

Yields: 6 - 8 serving(s)

Total Time: 10 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 1

    bottle sparkling rosé

  • 3 c.

    strawberry lemonade

  • 1/4 c.

    grenadine

  • 1

    sliced lemon

  • 1/2 c.

    sliced strawberries

  • Mint sprigs for garnish

Directions

  • Step 1Combine sparkling rosé, strawberry lemonade, grenadine, sliced lemon, and sliced strawberries into a large pitcher. Serve over ice and garnish with mint sprigs.
