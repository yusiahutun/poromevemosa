FIFA 20 TOTW 4 COUNTDOWN: Team of the Week Reveal, new FUT cards, Ultimate Team latest

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 1, 2024

FIFA 20 TOTW 4 will be revealed later today, introducing the fourth exciting FUT cards update to Ultimate Team mode.

As they do every week, EA Sports will announce the next Team of the Week squad at 3pm UK time on the dot.

The announcement made from EA Sports several social accounts, including Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram. That said, we imagine the FIFA 20 Reddit and official forum will also have the news fairly sharpish.

Like every week, players will be able to unpack these new FUT cards from 6pm UK time, when they replace the existing cards available from those players selected last week in the FIFA 20 TOTW 3 squad.

As we've covered previously, FUT players will receive a ratings boost to reflect their improved real-world performances in the last round of fixtures.

So it's likely that Premier League stars such as Adama Traore, who tore Manchester City to shreds, will be one of those in line for a FUT upgrade.

It's not science though, and you can never 100% predict what EA Sports decide to do.

Curious to know who FUThead believes will be chosen in the next FIFA 20 TOTW 4 squad? No problem, we've pulled together everything you need to know in this FIFA 20 TOTW 4 Predictions piece.

As for now, stay tuned, we'll have all the updates and announcements throughout today.
