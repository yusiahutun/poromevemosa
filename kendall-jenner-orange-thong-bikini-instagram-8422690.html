Kendall Jenner Wore an Itty-Bitty Orange Thong Bikini in Her First Swimsuit Pic of the Year

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 25, 2024

We're not even a week into the new year, and Kendall Jenner is already off to a sexy sartorial start. After kicking off 2024 with a nipple-baring sheer cutout gown while drinking wine on the beach during her Barbadian vacation, the supermodel debuted her first bikini of the year — and it's fiery in more ways than one. 

On Thursday, Jenner posted a new photo dump from her tropical getaway on Instagram, and in the snaps, she wore a micro orange "knitkini" from Deparel that included a teeny-tiny halter top and matching thong bottoms with string ties. Both pieces featured a white ruffled wave-like print that was a nod to the ocean in the background, while its fire-orange hue matched the sunset behind her. Kendall went barefoot, and accessorized with chunky gold earrings and a giant diamond ring on her index finger. 

Kendall's glam was minimal, as she wore little-to-no makeup and pulled back her dark brunette hair into a sleek ponytail with a center part.

Joining Kendall for her beach vacation were Hailey and Justin Bieber, as well as her ex-boyfriend Bad Bunny (born: Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio). But that doesn't mean the pair are rekindling their romance. According to a source at People, Jenner and Bunny, who reportedly split last month, "celebrated the New Year together with friends near Barbados," adding, however, that they were "not on the same flight" and are "not back together."
