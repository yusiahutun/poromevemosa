Michelle Keegan looks unreal in thigh-high boots and leopard print mini

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, October 4, 2024

The Brassic star shared a behind-the-scenes photo on Instagram

    Michelle Keegan braved the cold in a pair of thigh-high boots and leopard print mini in a behind-the-scenes snap on Instagram – and she looked unreal!

    The actress took some time out from filming her Sky One show, to brave the elements for a quick pic.
