Rhythm City welcomes Banda Maguey to the Event Center on May 31.

Hailing from Jalisco, Mexico, Banda Maguey was formed in Villa Corona and has been called one of the most successful exponents of the ‘technobanda’ sound. Led by singer Ernesto Solano, who composed several of their songs, including ‘Pero Te Amo,’ a hit from their fourth album, ‘El Mundo Gira,’ in 1998, the group signed a record deal with BMG U.S. Latin and recorded ‘Lágrimas De Sangre.’

A favorite during the 1990s banda craze, Banda Maguey performs Friday, May 31 at 8:00 p.m. in the Event Center at Rhythm City Casino Resort, located at 7077 Elmore Ave., Davenport. Tickets are available here or at The Market at Rhythm City. For more information, click here.
