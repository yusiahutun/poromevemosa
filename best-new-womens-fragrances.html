New perfumes for women 2024

Chanel Chance Eau Fraîche Eau De Parfum

Chanel Chance Eau Fraîche Eau De Parfum

As its title suggests, this sparkling, floral fragrance brings a fresh new cloak to Chanel's cult favourite Chance perfume. Its playful hue is also delightfully spring-ready.

Serge Lutens La Fille Tour de Fer Eau de Parfum

Serge Lutens La Fille Tour de Fer Eau de Parfum

Combining strong, feminine notes of pink berry essence, Turkish and Bulgarian rose and powdery iris, this decadent new fragrance draws its inspiration from rebellious and timeless Parisian style.

Maison Margiela Replica From The Garden Eau De Toilette

Maison Margiela Replica From The Garden Eau De Toilette

Looking for a springtime bloom without the stereotypical sweetness? This lightweight scent borrows its fresh greenness from tomato leaf, green mandarin, and earthy patchouli.

Kilian Paris Sunkissed Goddess Eau de Parfum

Kilian Paris Sunkissed Goddess Eau de Parfum

Like sunshine bottled, this gilded new perfume draws inspiration directly from sweltering summer rays. Combining solstice florals with creamy coconut and cocooning woods, this beachy array of notes is perfect for far-flung retreats.

Narciso Rodriguez for Her Musc Nude Eau de Parfum

This feminine musk fragrance is forever a cult classic and its latest edition, Musc Nude, is no exception. Featuring notes of jasmine, rose and tonka bean, it's elegant and sensual all at once.

Jo Malone London Ginger Beer Cologne

Jo Malone London Ginger Beer Cologne

This sparklingly crisp refreshment is effortlessly wearable and ideal for cosy, sunset evenings. Aromatic, sweet and spicy at all once, we recommend this scent for gourmand fans looking for a sophisticated seasonal update.

Dior Miss Dior Parfum

This elegant floral bouquet will always be a favourite of the Bazaar beauty team, and for good reason. It is feminine, flowery and daintily balanced with gentle fruity facets.

Dries Van Noten Mystic Moss

Dries Van Noten Mystic Moss

For fans of a true green scent, we recommend the new Dries Van Noten Mystic Moss. Described as an updated version of the brand's coveted Cannabis Patchouli, this vibrant perfume offers up a platter of green mandarin, clary sage, algae, oakmoss, vetiver and incense aromas.

Issey Miyake L'eau DIssey Solar Violet

This sunkissed floral scent is as playful in scent as it is in design. Watery fresh and lightly fruity, here you'll find notes of solar pear, violet leaf absolute and powdery iris.
