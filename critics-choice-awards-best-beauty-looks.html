Critics Choice Awards 2024: the best beauty looks

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 18, 2024

Emily Blunt

Michael Tran//Getty Images

Emily Blunt matched her make-up to her Armani Privé Couture dress which was also mirrored in her hair – her low bun nodding to the striking corsage shoulder detail.

Elizabeth Debicki

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Elizabeth Debicki demonstrated the enduring power of the smoky eye paired with nude lips and trailing waves.

Calah Lane

Christopher Polk//Getty Images

Calah Lane looked like art with her spherical hairstyle by Dionne Smith.

Emma Stone

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Emma Stone's ballerina bun was a welcome change to the low sleek chignons that often dominate red carpets.

Geffri Maya

Emma McIntyre//Getty Images

Margot Robbie

Lionel Hahn//Getty Images

Margot Robbie's undone ponytail was as romantic in energy as her custom Balmain gown.

Ariana Greenblatt

Frazer Harrison//Getty Images

Ariana Greenblatt's hair was also an extension of her outfit; her braids bound with matching leather.

Greta Lee

Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

Greta Lee – wearing Merit beauty – proved the place of wet-look hair on an awards ceremony carpet, her androgynous bob being the perfect parter to a sequin trouser suit.

Jennifer Aniston

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Jennifer Aniston's new long bob was tousled to perfection and her make-up was classically Aniston, featuring black eye make-up and nudy-pink lips.

Rachel Brosnahan

Rachel Brosnahan too chose a smoky eye, but paired hers with brown lipstick for a subtle modern update.
