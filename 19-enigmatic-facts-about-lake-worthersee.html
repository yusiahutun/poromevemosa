Curious Ancient Origins

Lake Wörthersee is believed to be over 25,000 years old, dating back to the last ice age. It was formed by the retreating glaciers and has since become a natural gem of Austria, captivating locals and tourists alike.

FAQs

1. How deep is Lake Wörthersee?

The maximum depth of Lake Wörthersee is approximately 85 meters (279 feet).

2. Can you swim in Lake Wörthersee?

Yes, swimming is allowed in Lake Wörthersee. The lake offers numerous public bathing areas and designated swimming spots.

3. Are there any water sports available on Lake Wörthersee?

Absolutely! Lake Wörthersee is a popular destination for water sports enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy activities like sailing, windsurfing, stand-up paddleboarding, and water skiing.

Yes, fishing is permitted in Lake Wörthersee. However, a fishing license is required, and there are regulations in place to protect the lake’s ecosystem and fish species.

8. Are there accommodations available near Lake Wörthersee?
