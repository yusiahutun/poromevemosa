Nicki Minaj, Ariana Grande, Pitbull, Nas

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 29, 2024

This week's #NewMusicFriday is packed -- and we mean packed -- with 21 awesome songs to get you ready for the weekend.

Besties Nicki Minaj and Ariana Grande teamed up on the duo's latest collaboration, "Bed" -- the first song for the latter since her bombshell engagement to Pete Davidson took over the internet. Nicki also put out another single, "Rich Sex," and the cover art has us drooling.

Pitbull also brought the heat with a new track, "Amore," featuring Leona Lewis. Powerhouse vocalist Alessia Cara dropped a new single -- "Growing Pains," as did Bebe Rexha -- "I'm a Mess."

Elsewhere, Nas released his 11th studio album, the Kanye-produced "Nasir," which is the rapper's birth name. "Nasir" marks the latest in a stack of high-profile G.O.O.D Music releases, produced by Mr. West.

In the rock arena, Young the Giant returned with "Simplify," The Kooks unveiled "Fractured and Dazed," Death Cab for Cutie released "Gold Rush," Houndmouth dropped new single "Modern Love" and Kodaline may have another hit on their hands with "Shed a Tear."
