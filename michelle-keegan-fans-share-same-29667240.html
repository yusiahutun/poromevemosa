Michelle Keegan fans share same complaint as she shows off massive sofa with Mark Wright

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Michelle Keegan fans have all shared the same complaint as her husband Mark Wright shared a snap of their stunning home.

TOWIE star and TV personality Mark has been married to former Coronation Street actress Michelle since 2015, with the couple living in a £3.5m Essex mansion.

Mark took to Instagram to share a photograph of the couple's enormous lounge at the mansion, which includes a gigantic cream sofa that spills around the room.

The lounge is furnished with a brown coffee table, white paneled walls and a huge chandelier above the sofa - along with pampas grass, a chic lamp and photographs adorning a side table.

He captioned the beautiful snap: "Scatter cushions finishing our living room sofa perfectly."

However, fans took to the comments section to share the same complaint as they said the lounge looked more like a "show room."

"It's stunning but not homely more like a show room," said one fan.

A second agreed: "Perfection but that’s not living, won’t want to sit on the sofa for fear of messing it up. Every house needs to be a home not a show."

"It’s a show house not a home," added a third.

A fourth penned: "Stunning but looks unlived in."

The couple have been renovating their home and sharing the photographs on social media through a dedicated page for their mansion.

They previously shared an outside snap of their home as they gushed about the landscape gardening, with the couple saying: "Our garden means the world to us and we love the outdoors."

Mark and Michelle continued: "It's funny how you can have a dream, tell the professionals and they make your dream bigger than you ever imagined."

One of the latest spots to be renovated at their home is the new gym space, which includes a wall of mirrors from floor to ceiling, as well as being filled with equipment including weights.

Mark showcased a before and after video of their home gym, as he dressed in workout gear while smiling at one of the mirrors and flexing his muscles.

He captioned their latest renovation post: "Absolutely buzzing to have the gym completed.

"The arrival of the equipment today has made us very happy!"
